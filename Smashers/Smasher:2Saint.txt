   2saint is an American [21]Jigglypuff main from New York who is currently ranked 4th on the [22]NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings and 49th on the [23]2018 MPGR. He has wins over [24]Lucky, [25]The Moon, [26]HugS, [27]MikeHaze, [28]Gahtzu, [29]Slox, [30]Squid and [31]DJ Nintendo.

   At [32]CEO Dreamland, 2saint had a 3-0 win against Gahtzu, earning him 13th place at his first major. About a year later, he followed this up with a strong 17th place finish at [33]Full Bloom 4, defeating both MikeHaze and Lucky 3-0 before losing to [34]Ginger 2-3. After this performance, 2saint was voted into [35]Smash Summit 6, where he defeated [36]HugS.
