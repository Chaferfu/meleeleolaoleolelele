   2saint is an American Jigglypuff main from New York who is currently ranked 4th on the NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings and 49th on the 2018 MPGR. He has wins over Lucky, The Moon, HugS, MikeHaze, Gahtzu, Slox, Squid and DJ Nintendo.

   At CEO Dreamland, 2saint had a 3-0 win against Gahtzu, earning him 13th place at his first major. About a year later, he followed this up with a strong 17th place finish at Full Bloom 4, defeating both MikeHaze and Lucky 3-0 before losing to Ginger 2-3. After this performance, 2saint was voted into Smash Summit 6, where he defeated HugS.
