   Melee main Fox
      Sponsor(s)    Kyoto eSports
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
       Ranking      Super Smash Bros. Melee Texas Power Rankings: 4th
                             Additional info
       Location     Rio Grande Valley, Texas  United States

   ALP is a top Melee player from deep South Texas who mains Fox. He is currently ranked 4th in the Melee Texas Power Rankings and 1st in the Rio Grande Valley Power Rankings. He occasionally travels around Texas and places well at locals and regionals.

   On May 28th, 2017, ALP was signed in by Kyoto eSports, alongside Bizzarro Flame and Redd.
