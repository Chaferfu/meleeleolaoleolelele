   ALP is a top [13]Melee player from deep South Texas who mains [14]Fox. He is currently ranked 4th in the Melee Texas Power Rankings and 1st in the Rio Grande Valley Power Rankings. He occasionally travels around Texas and places well at locals and regionals.

   On May 28th, 2017, ALP [15]was signed in by Kyoto eSports, alongside [16]Bizzarro Flame and [17]Redd.
