   ARMY is a Melee Ice Climbers main from Long Beach, California currently residing in Newbury Park, California, and is currently considered to be one of the two best Ice Climbers player in the world along with Bananas. He is currently ranked 2nd on the SoCal Melee Power Rankings and 20th on the 2018 MPGR. He has had strong showings at nationals such as 4th at Low Tier City 6 and 5th at Super Smash Con 2018. ARMY has wins over top players such as Mango, Axe, Zain, Westballz, Ice, MikeHaze, HugS, Rishi, Android, Captain Faceroll, and Ryan Ford. In Project M, he has wins over players such as Aero.
