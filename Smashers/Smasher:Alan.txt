   Alan is a Peach/Sheik/Marth user from San Jose, California currently residing in Santa Barbara, California who is well known for his doubles play. He routinely teams with Shroomed and other top NorCal players. He is currently ranked 21st on the SoCal Melee Power Rankings and the NorCal Melee Power Rankings.
