                           Albert
   Melee main Falco
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                      Additional info
       Location     DFW, Texas  USA

   Albert is a Melee Falco main from Texas. He is known for his highly technical Falco play, despite being relatively new. He is notorious for beating top players in his region, including MacD and Wobbles in close matches, in just his first year of competitive Smash. He was previously ranked 2nd on the Central Texas Power Rankings.
