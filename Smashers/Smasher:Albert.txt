                           Albert
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Falco
        Skill       [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                      Additional info
       Location     DFW, Texas [10] USA

   Albert is a [11]Melee [12]Falco main from Texas. He is known for his highly technical Falco play, despite being relatively new. He is notorious for beating top players in his region, including [13]MacD and [14]Wobbles in close matches, in just his first year of competitive Smash. He was previously ranked 2nd on the [15]Central Texas Power Rankings.
