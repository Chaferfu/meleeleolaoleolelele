   Alex19 is a smasher from Norwalk, California who mains Fox. He was active around 2009 and 2011 but played sporadically for the next few years. Since 2013 he has become more active. He is currently ranked 18th on the SoCal Melee Power Rankings. He has beaten many notable players such as Silent Wolf, Lucky, S2J, HugS, Wizzrobe, and Zhu.

   On March 7th, 2017 he signed with Weedmaps^.
