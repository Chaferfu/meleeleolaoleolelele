   Android is a [27]smasher from Gothensburg, Sweden who mains red [28]Sheik. He is [29]Aniolas and [30]Armada's younger brother. He regularly teams with Armada when they travel to America and their greatest accomplishments were winning [31]The Big House 5 and [32]GENESIS 4 in the teams events. He is currently ranked 3rd on the [33]Swedish Melee Power Rankings, 8th on the [34]European Melee Power Rankings, and 66th on the [35]2018 MPGR.
