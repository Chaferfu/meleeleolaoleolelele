   aperture is a professional smasher in the United Kingdom, who mains Sonic in Smash 4. He is ranked 5th in the Smash 4 England Power Rankings and is one of the strongest Sonic players in the UK and Europe along with Ixis who is ranked 1st on the Smash 4 England Power Rankings. His notable feats come from his performances at B.E.A.S.T 7 in which he got 7th and 2GGC: Nairo Saga where he placed 25th while defeating Tweek and ANTi along the way. He also has notable wins over J.Miller, Meru and Ixis himself.
