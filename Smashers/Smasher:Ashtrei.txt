                             Ashtrei
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Sheik
        Skill       [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee Semi-professional
                         Additional info
      Real name     Joakim Karlsson
       Location     Stockholm [10] Sweden

   Ashtrei is a Swedish [11]Melee [12]Sheik main. He is ranked 7th on the [13]Stockholm Power Rankings and 13th on the [14]Swedish Power Rankings. He has wins over players such as [15]Daydee, [16]Humpe, and [17]Rocky. He's one of three brothers who'se all been top 20 at one point in the Stockholm Power Rankings.
