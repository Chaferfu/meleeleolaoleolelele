                             Ashtrei
   Melee main Sheik
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Semi-professional
                         Additional info
      Real name     Joakim Karlsson
       Location     Stockholm  Sweden

   Ashtrei is a Swedish Melee Sheik main. He is ranked 7th on the Stockholm Power Rankings and 13th on the Swedish Power Rankings. He has wins over players such as Daydee, Humpe, and Rocky. He's one of three brothers who'se all been top 20 at one point in the Stockholm Power Rankings.
