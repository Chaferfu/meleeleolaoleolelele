   nebbii, formerly known as Avalancer, is a Yoshi main from Nootdorp, The Netherlands and is considered the best Yoshi player in Europe. He is currently ranked 2nd on the Dutch Melee Power Rankings, 10th on the European Melee Power Rankings, and 98th on the 2018 MPGR. He has taken sets from players such as Amsah, Professor Pro, Android, Vanity Angel and Tekk, and has taken a game off Armada.
