   Azusa is a Melee smasher from Northern California who mains Peach. He is currently ranked 6th on the NorCal Melee Power Rankings and 59th on the 2017 SSBMRank. He has beaten smashers such as Shroomed, Nintendude, HugS, Laudandus, Cactuar, Captain Faceroll, and Ralph.
