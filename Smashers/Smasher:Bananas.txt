   Bananas is a Melee Ice Climbers main from Dallas, Texas who is considered to be one of the two best Ice Climbers players in the world along with ARMY, and is currently ranked 1st on the Central Texas Melee Power Rankings and 19th on the 2018 MPGR. Notably, he has had victories over several top 100 players, namely Plup, Mew2King, Mango, Axe, S2J, n0ne, HugS, Swedish Delight, Syrox, and Abate.
