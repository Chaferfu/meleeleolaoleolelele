   Baxon is a French [16]Falco player who lives in Paris/Avignon. He is considered to be one of the best Melee Falco players in Europe (along with [17]Calle W) and has placed very highly at European national tournaments. Baxon is currently ranked 4th on the French Melee Power Rankings and 28th on the [18]European Melee Power Rankings.
