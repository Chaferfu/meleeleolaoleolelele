   Baxon is a French Falco player who lives in Paris/Avignon. He is considered to be one of the best Melee Falco players in Europe (along with Calle W) and has placed very highly at European national tournaments. Baxon is currently ranked 4th on the French Melee Power Rankings and 28th on the European Melee Power Rankings.
