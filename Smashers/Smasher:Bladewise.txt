   Bladewise is an American [37]smasher located in the Washington state, known for being one of the best [38]Melee [39]Peach mains in the USA along with [40]lloD and [41]Kalamazhu. He is a major competitor in Washington tournaments, and has made decent impacts at large nationals, such as [42]Apex 2013, [43]EVO 2013, [44]Apex 2015, [45]EVO 2016 and [46]The Big House 6.

   He is currently ranked 1st on the [47]Washington Melee Power Rankings and 44th on the [48]2018 MPGR.
