   Blea Gelo is a smasher originally from Venezuela now residing in Miami, Florida since 2009. He is considered to be one of the best Melee Luigi players in the world. He has taken sets off of notable players such as Wizzrobe, Ice, Mike Haze, Druggedfox, Porkchops, Ryan Ford, Gravy, Gahtzu, Bladewise, and Chillin. He is currently ranked 2nd on the South Florida Melee Power Rankings, 7th on the South Florida Project M Power Rankings, and 90th on the 2017 SSBMRank.

Triviaedit

     * Blea Gelo is the second Luigi main to make Top 8 at a national Melee tournament, the first being Abate.
     * Blea Gelo is also known for creating the Florida "Olé" chant for smash that can be heard at many tournaments in and out of state.
     * At Smash Summit 5, Blea Gelo became the first Luigi main to participate in a Smash Summit event.
