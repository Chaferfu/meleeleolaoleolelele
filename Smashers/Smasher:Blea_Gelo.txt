   Blea Gelo is a [28]smasher originally from Venezuela now residing in Miami, Florida since 2009. He is considered to be one of the best [29]Melee [30]Luigi players in the world. He has taken sets off of notable players such as [31]Wizzrobe, [32]Ice, [33]Mike Haze, [34]Druggedfox, [35]Porkchops, [36]Ryan Ford, [37]Gravy, [38]Gahtzu, [39]Bladewise, and [40]Chillin. He is currently ranked 2nd on the [41]South Florida Melee Power Rankings, 7th on the South Florida [42]Project M Power Rankings, and 90th on the [43]2017 SSBMRank.

Trivia[[136]edit]

     * Blea Gelo is the second [137]Luigi main to make Top 8 at a national [138]Melee tournament, the first being [139]Abate.
     * Blea Gelo is also known for creating the Florida "Olé" chant for smash that can be heard at many tournaments in and out of state.
     * At [140]Smash Summit 5, Blea Gelo became the first Luigi main to participate in a Smash Summit event.
