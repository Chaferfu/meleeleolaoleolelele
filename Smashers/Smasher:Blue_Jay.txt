   Blue Jay is a Fox main from Salt Lake City, Utah. He is currently ranked 3rd on the Utah Power Rankings and was an initial nominee on the 2017 SSBMRank. He got 33rd at Utah national GameTyrant Expo 2017, and 49th at Texas national DreamHack Austin 2017. He has wins over #1 in Utah Logos, Perfect Chaos, Bizzarro Flame, OkayP., and Ka-Master.
