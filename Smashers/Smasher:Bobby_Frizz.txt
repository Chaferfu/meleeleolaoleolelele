   Bobby Frizz is a Melee Fox main from Olathe, Kansas who is currently residing in Kansas City, Missouri. At EVO 2016, he placed 33rd after losing to PewPewU and Frootloop. He has won various sets over Darkrain and won a set over Lucky at The Big House 7. He is also a very proficient Project M player and is considered to be one of the best active Fox players in the world. Bobby Frizz is currently ranked 1st on the Kansas Melee Power Rankings and 36th on the PMRank 2018, and was formerly ranked 92nd on the 2017 SSBMRank.

   At GENESIS 4, he beat Santiago in 2nd round of pools to clinch a 65th placing. At Run, Don't Walk he beat Captain Faceroll in winners semis to get a second place finish.
