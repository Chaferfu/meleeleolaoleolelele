      Real name     Jonah Voegtly
       Location     Northern California  United States

   Boulevard is a Melee Luigi player from Northern California. He was formerly ranked 14th on the NorCal Melee Power Rankings. He has wins over players such as PewPewU and dizzkidboogie.
