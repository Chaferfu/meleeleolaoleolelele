   Boyd is a Melee Ice Climbers main from Northeastern Ohio. He is currently ranked 1st on the Northeastern Ohio Melee Power Rankings and 67th on the 2018 MPGR. He notably beat Amsah at Smash'n'Splash 4 and n0ne at Show Me Your Moves 19.
