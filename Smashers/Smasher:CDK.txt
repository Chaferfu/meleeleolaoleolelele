   CDK, also known as ConnorDaKid or simply Connor, is a [22]Fox and [23]Sheik player from Garden Grove, California. He was formerly ranked 13th on the [24]SoCal Melee Power Rankings. He is also one of the founders of MIOM Conspiracy Theory. CDK was signed by True Ambition on March 8th of 2016.
