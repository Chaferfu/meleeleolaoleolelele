   CDK, also known as ConnorDaKid or simply Connor, is a Fox and Sheik player from Garden Grove, California. He was formerly ranked 13th on the SoCal Melee Power Rankings. He is also one of the founders of MIOM Conspiracy Theory. CDK was signed by True Ambition on March 8th of 2016.
