                                          CIZ "C!Z"
                 Melee main Marth
   Other Melee
   character Fox
                      Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
                                       Additional info
                    Real name     TC Clark
                     Location     New Jersey  United States

   CIZ is a strong active Melee Marth in the Tristate area. He is ranked 1st on the New Jersey Melee Power Rankings. He routinely places in top 3 at local weeklies and biweeklies. Originally from Washington, he was formerly ranked on the Washington Melee Power Rankings.
