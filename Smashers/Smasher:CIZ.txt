                 [7]Melee [8]main [9]Marth
   Other [10]Melee
   [11]character [12]Fox
                      Skill       [13]Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
                                       Additional info
                    Real name     TC Clark
                     Location     New Jersey [14] United States

   CIZ is a strong active [15]Melee [16]Marth in the Tristate area. He is ranked 1st on the [17]New Jersey Melee Power Rankings. He routinely places in top 3 at local weeklies and biweeklies. Originally from Washington, he was formerly ranked on the [18]Washington Melee Power Rankings.
