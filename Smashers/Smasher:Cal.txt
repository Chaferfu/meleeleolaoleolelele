   Cal, formerly known as Resi and Sephy, is a Melee Fox and Sheik main from Cincinnati, Ohio who is currently ranked 1st on the Cincinnati Melee Power Rankings and 87th on the 2018 MPGR. He rose into prominence by winning the Forsaken Bracket at Red Bull Smash Gods and Gatekeepers by consecutively upsetting multiple top players including NMW, Captain Faceroll, Bizzarro Flame, Drephen, and Azusa, earning him the nickname "King of the Forsaken".
