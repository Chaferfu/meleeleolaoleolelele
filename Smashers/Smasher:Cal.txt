   Cal, formerly known as Resi and Sephy, is a Melee [17]Fox and [18]Sheik main from Cincinnati, Ohio who is currently ranked 1st on the Cincinnati Melee Power Rankings and 87th on the [19]2018 MPGR. He rose into prominence by winning the Forsaken Bracket at [20]Red Bull Smash Gods and Gatekeepers by consecutively upsetting multiple top players including [21]NMW, [22]Captain Faceroll, [23]Bizzarro Flame, [24]Drephen, and [25]Azusa, earning him the nickname "King of the Forsaken".
