   Captain Smuckers also known as Smuckers, Smucks, or related variations (such as Smuck him Up) is one of the stronger [22]Melee smashers in the [23]Tristate Area from New York. He is a flashy [24]Captain Falcon main with a [25]Fox secondary. He usually places top 8 at Nebulous, a top level weekly tournament in Chinatown, NYC and has beaten many strong smashers including [26]Nintendude, [27]KirbyKaze, [28]MacD, [29]Slox, [30]Crush, and [31]Zain. He has hosted Melee tournaments in Westchester and other areas of New York. He got a very respectable 13th at [32]RoM 7 and has also gotten other high placings at other major New York events.

   Smuckers is currently ranked 7th on the [33]NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings and 42nd on the [34]2018 MPGR. He was previously ranked 12th on the [35]NY/NJ Project M Power Rankings.
