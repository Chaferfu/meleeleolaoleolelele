   Captain Smuckers also known as Smuckers, Smucks, or related variations (such as Smuck him Up) is one of the stronger Melee smashers in the Tristate Area from New York. He is a flashy Captain Falcon main with a Fox secondary. He usually places top 8 at Nebulous, a top level weekly tournament in Chinatown, NYC and has beaten many strong smashers including Nintendude, KirbyKaze, MacD, Slox, Crush, and Zain. He has hosted Melee tournaments in Westchester and other areas of New York. He got a very respectable 13th at RoM 7 and has also gotten other high placings at other major New York events.

   Smuckers is currently ranked 7th on the NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings and 42nd on the 2018 MPGR. He was previously ranked 12th on the NY/NJ Project M Power Rankings.
