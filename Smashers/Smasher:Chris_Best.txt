                            Chris Best
   Melee main Marth
      Crew(s)    Twitch, Berkeley
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Semi-professional
                         Additional info
      Real name     Chris Best
       Location     San Francisco  USA

   Chris Best is a Northern Californian Marth main who entered the scene in 2015 and works at Twitch. He is currently ranked 17th on the NorCal Melee Power Rankings with wins over Nintendude, Ralph, HomeMadeWaffles, Phil, Darrell, and Kira.
