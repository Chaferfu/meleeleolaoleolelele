      Crew(s)    Spire
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
       Ranking      Super Smash Bros. Melee 2018 MPGR: 94th
                          Additional info
      Real name     Jaycob Applegate
       Location     Kentucky  United States

   Cob is a Melee Peach main from Kentucky. He has beaten players such as Colbol, Rik, Lucky, Abate, Reeve, and Nightmare. He is currently ranked 2nd on the Kentucky Melee Power Rankings and 94th on the 2018 MPGR.
