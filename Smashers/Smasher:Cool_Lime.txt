   Additional info
   Real name Diego Wheat
   Location Falls Church, Virginia [14] United States

   Cool Lime is an [15]Ice Climbers player from Virginia. He was formerly ranked 10th on the [16]MD/VA Melee Power Rankings, and is a Hidden Boss on the [17]2017 SSBMRank. He has tournament wins over [18]Zain, [19]Captain Smuckers, [20]Junebug, [21]2Saint, [22]Overtriforce, [23]Drephen, [24]Jerry, [25]Vist, [26]HTwa, and [27]Kounotori.
