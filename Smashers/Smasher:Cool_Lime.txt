   Cool Lime
   Melee main Ice Climbers
   SSB64 main Captain Falcon
   Skill Super Smash Bros. Semi-Professional
   Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
   Additional info
   Real name Diego Wheat
   Location Falls Church, Virginia  United States

   Cool Lime is an Ice Climbers player from Virginia. He was formerly ranked 10th on the MD/VA Melee Power Rankings, and is a Hidden Boss on the 2017 SSBMRank. He has tournament wins over Zain, Captain Smuckers, Junebug, 2Saint, Overtriforce, Drephen, Jerry, Vist, HTwa, and Kounotori.
