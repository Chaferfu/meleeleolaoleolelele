   Darktooth is a Melee player from Long Island, New York, and mains Fox in [18]Melee and [19]Project M. He is best known for coining and discovering [20]shine mines, a technique usable by Fox and Falco in Melee. He is currently ranked 1st on the Long Island Melee Power Rankings, 5th on the [21]NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings, and 59th on the [22]2018 MPGR. Considered to be the top Long Island Melee player rarely dropping tournaments in region, he boasts wins over [23]KJH, [24]ChuDat, [25]Amsah, [26]Cactuar, [27]Junebug, [28]Redd, [29]Zamu and [30]Lovage at various majors, in addition to wins over [31]Hax$, [32]Captain Smuckers, and nearly the rest of Tristate at locals/regionals.
