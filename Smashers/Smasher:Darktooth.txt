   Darktooth is a Melee player from Long Island, New York, and mains Fox in Melee and Project M. He is best known for coining and discovering shine mines, a technique usable by Fox and Falco in Melee. He is currently ranked 1st on the Long Island Melee Power Rankings, 5th on the NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings, and 59th on the 2018 MPGR. Considered to be the top Long Island Melee player rarely dropping tournaments in region, he boasts wins over KJH, ChuDat, Amsah, Cactuar, Junebug, Redd, Zamu and Lovage at various majors, in addition to wins over Hax$, Captain Smuckers, and nearly the rest of Tristate at locals/regionals.
