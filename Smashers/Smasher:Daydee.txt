   Daydee is a Swedish Melee Samus main. He is considered to be the best Samus player in Europe. He is currently ranked 2nd on the Stockholm Power Rankings, 4th on the Swedish Power Rankings, and 15th on the European Melee Power Rankings. He has notably beaten dizzkidboogie at B.E.A.S.T 7 and Professor Pro at Heir 5. His favorite pizza is Margarita due to its "value".
