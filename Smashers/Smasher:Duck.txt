   Duck is a [23]Samus main from Ann Arbor, Michigan who is considered to be one of the best Samus players in the world, along with [24]Plup and [25]HugS, with wins against other top players such as [26]Hungrybox, [27]Leffen, and [28]Westballz under his belt. He is known for his safe and calculated style of play, getting 5th at [29]The Big House and beating HugS, in pools at [30]Kings of Cali 3. He also managed to take two sets off of [31]KirbyKaze, at [32]SWEET XIV and [33]The Come Up 2014, as well as eliminating Leffen from [34]DreamHack Winter 2015. He placed 13th at [35]EVO 2015 and 9th at [36]The Big House 5. His most notable performance was [37]DreamHack Winter 2015 where he placed 5th and defeated [38]SFAT, [39]Wizzrobe, Leffen, and [40]MacD. Duck is currently ranked 1st on the [41]Michigan Melee Power Rankings and 14th on the [42]2018 MPGR.

   On August 15, 2016, Duck was signed onto [43]Denial eSports, but left on January 5th, 2017.

   On April 11, 2017, Duck was signed by [44]Phoenix1.^[45][1]

   On May 17, 2018, Duck announced he was no longer part of Phoenix1, thanking them for the support and opportunities they've given him.
