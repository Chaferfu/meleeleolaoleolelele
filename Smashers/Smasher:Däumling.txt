   Däumling is a [18]Melee player from Dortmund, Germany. He is considered to be the best [19]Ice Climbers player in Europe. Däumling is currently ranked 6th on the [20]German Melee Power Rankings and 26th on the [21]European Melee Power Rankings. He took a set off [22]Amsah.
