                        EastCoastJeff
   Melee main Fox
                       Additional info
      Real name     Jeffrey Yuan
       Location     San Diego, California  United States

   EastCoastJeff is a Fox main from SoCal. The reason why he is called EastCoastJeff is to avoid confusion with SilentSpectre after the former moved from Rhode Island to NorCal. He has taken sets off Mang0, Chillin, Shroomed, NMW, FendrickLamar, and many others. He is currently ranked 3rd on the San Diego Melee Power Rankings and 20th on the SoCal Melee Power Rankings.
