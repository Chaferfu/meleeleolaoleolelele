                        EastCoastJeff
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Fox
                       Additional info
      Real name     Jeffrey Yuan
       Location     San Diego, California [9] United States

   EastCoastJeff is a [10]Fox [11]main from [12]SoCal. The reason why he is called EastCoastJeff is to avoid confusion with [13]SilentSpectre after the former moved from Rhode Island to NorCal. He has taken sets off [14]Mang0, [15]Chillin, [16]Shroomed, [17]NMW, [18]FendrickLamar, and many others. He is currently ranked 3rd on the San Diego Melee Power Rankings and 20th on the [19]SoCal Melee Power Rankings.
