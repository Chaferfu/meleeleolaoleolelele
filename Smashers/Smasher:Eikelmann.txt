   Eikelmann is a Russian-American smasher, originally from Petrozavodsk, Russia, but now residing in Denver, Colorado who mains [37]Ganondorf in Melee but also plays [38]Falco, [39]Bowser and [40]Donkey Kong. He is considered to be one of the best Bowser and Ganondorf mains in the United States with his high level of [41]spacing and character knowledge. He has taken sets off of smashers such as [42]n0ne, [43]S2J, [44]ESAM, [45]s0ft, [46]Wizzrobe, [47]Leffen, and games off of [48]Hungrybox and [49]Mew2King. He remains one of three Ganondorf players (alongside [50]Kage and [51]WhatIsFear) to have taken a game off of [52]Armada's [53]Peach. He was formerly ranked 2nd on the [54]Colorado Power Rankings.

   Following allegations of abuse from former partners as well as misconduct at tournaments, Eikelmann announced via Facebook that he would enter an indefinite hiatus from competitive play.^[55][1] He has also been banned in Colorado following the allegations.
