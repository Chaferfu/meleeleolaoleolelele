                                   FA0 "FAO"
   Melee main Ice Climbers
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
       Ranking      Super Smash Bros. Melee Swedish Power Rankings: 8th
                                Additional info
      Real name     Robin Castlin
       Location     Stockholm  Sweden

   FA0 is a Swedish Melee Ice Climbers main and is considered one of the best Ice Climbers players in Europe. He is currently ranked 3rd on the Stockholm Power Rankings, 8th on the Swedish Power Rankings and 29th on the European Melee Power Rankings.
