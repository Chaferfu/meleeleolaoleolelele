                                   FA0 "FAO"
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Ice Climbers
        Skill       [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
       Ranking      [10]Super Smash Bros. Melee [11]Swedish Power Rankings: 8th
                                Additional info
      Real name     Robin Castlin
       Location     Stockholm [12] Sweden

   FA0 is a Swedish [13]Melee [14]Ice Climbers main and is considered one of the best Ice Climbers players in Europe. He is currently ranked 3rd on the [15]Stockholm Power Rankings, 8th on the [16]Swedish Power Rankings and 29th on the [17]European Melee Power Rankings.
