   FatGoku is a smasher from Oregon who is one of the top competitors in the Oregon/Pacific Northwest area. He is known for his patience in play. He has taken sets off of top players such as S2J, Syrox, Duck, and Bladewise. He is currently ranked 1st on the Oregon Power Rankings and 69th on the 2018 MPGR.
