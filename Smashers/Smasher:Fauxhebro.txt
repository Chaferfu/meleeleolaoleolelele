                          Fauxhebro
   Melee main Ice Climbers
      Crew(s)    Ribbon Large
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                       Additional info
      Real name     Chaucer Gilson
       Location     Vancouver, British Columbia  Canada

   Fauxhebro is a Melee Ice Climbers main from British Columbia, Canada and is one of the best Ice Climbers in the country. He has multiple wins on the PNW's and Canada's elite including Silent Wolf, Bladewise, FatGoku, Iceman, Rustin, Eggz, and Moky, RK, Star respectively. He is currently ranked 1st on the British Columbia Power Rankings.
