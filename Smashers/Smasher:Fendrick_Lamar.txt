   FendrickLamar is a Michigan-based Super Smash Bros. Melee commentator currently residing in Washington, D.C. He was formerly ranked 17th on the MD/VA Melee Power Rankings. He mainly commentates events in the Midwest and MD/VA regions as well as out-of-state events like EVO. In terms of playing, he is a Marth player, and has wins over Zain, SmashG0D, Ginger, Toph and s0ft.
