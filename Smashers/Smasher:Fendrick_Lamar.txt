   FendrickLamar is a Michigan-based [16]Super Smash Bros. Melee [17]commentator currently residing in Washington, D.C. He was formerly ranked 17th on the [18]MD/VA Melee Power Rankings. He mainly commentates events in the Midwest and MD/VA regions as well as out-of-state events like [19]EVO. In terms of playing, he is a [20]Marth player, and has wins over [21]Zain, [22]SmashG0D, [23]Ginger, [24]Toph and [25]s0ft.
