   Flippy is a Fox main from Ijmuiden, The Netherlands. He was formerly ranked 20th on the Dutch Power Rankings, He started playing Smash on January 2014. He retired in 2018 after Heir 5. Flippy's notable wins include nebbii, Chrollo, Chompsky/r0cket_wizard,Atlas, Skills, Ryuker, PureEvil, Enig, KasparV, Epikfaal, Jim Morrison, seo, skullbro, Nuradin, Razor Ramon, JohsyJam and Toa. Flippy also played Project M on the side and managed to take a set off a PR'd player Vies.
