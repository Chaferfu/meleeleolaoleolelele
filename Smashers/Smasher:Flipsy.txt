   Flipsy is a smasher from Naples, Florida who mains Ice Climbers in Melee. He is currently ranked 1st on both the Southwest Florida and South Florida Melee Power Rankings and 90th on the 2018 MPGR. He is considered to be a high level player in Florida who has caused many upsets in the past year. Flipsy has won sets against Mango, DJ Nintendo, Gravy, and A Rookie. He also placed an impressive 17th at CEO Dreamland and 9th at Frame Perfect Series 2.
