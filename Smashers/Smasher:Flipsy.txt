   Flipsy is a smasher from Naples, Florida who mains [17]Ice Climbers in [18]Melee. He is currently ranked 1st on both the Southwest Florida and [19]South Florida Melee Power Rankings and 90th on the [20]2018 MPGR. He is considered to be a high level player in Florida who has caused many upsets in the past year. Flipsy has won sets against [21]Mango, [22]DJ Nintendo, [23]Gravy, and [24]A Rookie. He also placed an impressive 17th at [25]CEO Dreamland and 9th at [26]Frame Perfect Series 2.
