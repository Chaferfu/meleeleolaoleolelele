                             Frenzy
   Melee main Falco
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
                        Additional info
      Real name     Elliot Grossman
       Location     Bristol  England

   Frenzy is a Falco main from Bristol, England, ranked 5th on the United Kingdom Melee Power Rankings and 35th on the European Melee Power Rankings. Best known for his tech skill and strong ledge play, he has victories on players such as Professor Pro, Overtriforce, Drunksloth, Daumling, HomeMadeWaffles, and Spud.
