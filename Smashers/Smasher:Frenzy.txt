                             Frenzy
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Falco
        Skill       [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
                        Additional info
      Real name     Elliot Grossman
       Location     Bristol [10] England

   Frenzy is a [11]Falco main from Bristol, England, ranked 5th on the [12]United Kingdom Melee Power Rankings and 35th on the [13]European Melee Power Rankings. Best known for his tech skill and strong ledge play, he has victories on players such as [14]Professor Pro, [15]Overtriforce, [16]Drunksloth, [17]Daumling, [18]HomeMadeWaffles, and [19]Spud.
