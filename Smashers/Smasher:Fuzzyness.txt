   Fuzzyness is a Fox and Captain Falcon player from London, currently ranked 4th on the United Kingdom Melee Power Rankings and 14th on the European Melee Power Rankings. He attends UK tournaments regularly and sorts out tournaments within London and regular smashfests, and performs quite well at nationals, placing high at nationals in both Europe and the United States. Fuzzyness's play with Captain Falcon became recognized in the European community when he took Armada to game 5 in a winners' bracket set at B.E.A.S.T II, and he later became known as an equally skilled Fox player. He has also managed to take sets off of players such as Ice, Nintendude, Dizzkidboogie, Overtriforce, Professor Pro, La Luna, Amsah, Rik and OkamiBW. He is also a noted speedrunner, having world records in categories such as Melee's Adventure Mode in the Normal and Very Hard difficulties.^
