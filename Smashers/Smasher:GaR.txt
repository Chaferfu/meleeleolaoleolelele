                             gaR
                            GaR
   Melee main Sheik
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                       Additional info
      Real name     Ivan Van
       Location     San Francisco, California  USA

   gaR is a smasher originally from Texas who now resides in NorCal who mains Sheik in Melee, and was formerly ranked 13th on the NorCal Melee Power Rankings. He is known to chaingrab in sheik ditto friendlies and is easily spotted at nationals due to his hair. gaR created the gaRPR smasher ranking system.
