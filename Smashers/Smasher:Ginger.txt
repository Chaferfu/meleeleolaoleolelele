                               Ginger
                              [6]Ginger
   [7]Melee [8]main [9]Falco
      Sponsor(s)    Balance Gaming
        Skill       [10]Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
       Ranking      [11]Super Smash Bros. Melee [12]2018 MPGR: 40th
                          Additional info
      Real name     Avery Wilson
       Location     Brighton, Michigan [13] United States

   Ginger is a [14]Falco main currently ranked 3rd on the [15]Michigan Melee Power Rankings and 40th on the [16]2018 MPGR. He is notable for unexpectedly defeating [17]Druggedfox at [18]The Big House 5 in Winners Semis of Round 1 Pools. He has also eliminated [19]Westballz at [20]The Big House 7 and beat [21]Axe at [22]Full Bloom 4. After his breakout performances in 2017, Ginger became an active twitch streamer and is known for playing high level anther's ladder matches and for his catchphrase "Zoinks" onstream.
