   Gravy is a Captain Falcon main in Melee from New York who currently resides in Orlando, Florida. He is currently ranked 4th on the Central Florida Melee Power Rankings. He has beaten notable players such as KoreanDJ, Ken, Kels, Axe, and DruggedFox. He is currently the Melee representative for Hitbox, a company that creates arcade-esque controllers for fighting games such as Street Fighter V and soon, Super Smash Bros.^.

Triviaedit

     * Gravy is best known for coming back from a 4-stock to 1-stock deficit against KoreanDJ's Marth on Dream Land, surviving to 241%, defeating him 2-0 at NSA 2.
     * Gravy is a member of the 20GX group along with Wizzrobe and Gahtzu, which focuses on pushing Captain Falcon's metagame.
