   Gravy is a [16]Captain Falcon main in [17]Melee from New York who currently resides in Orlando, Florida. He is currently ranked 4th on the [18]Central Florida Melee Power Rankings. He has beaten notable players such as [19]KoreanDJ, [20]Ken, [21]Kels, [22]Axe, and [23]DruggedFox. He is currently the Melee representative for [24]Hitbox, a company that creates arcade-esque controllers for fighting games such as Street Fighter V and soon, Super Smash Bros.^[25][1].

Trivia[[79]edit]

     * Gravy is best known for coming back from a 4-stock to 1-stock deficit against KoreanDJ's Marth on Dream Land, surviving to 241%, defeating him 2-0 at [80]NSA 2.
     * Gravy is a member of the 20GX group along with [81]Wizzrobe and [82]Gahtzu, which focuses on pushing Captain Falcon's metagame.
