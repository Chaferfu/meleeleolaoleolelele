   HTwa is a Sheik main from Atlanta, Georgia.

   He has taken sets off of n0ne, Slox, $mike, KPAN, Ginger, and Crush.

   He is currently ranked 2nd on the Melee Georgia Power Rankings and 81st on the 2018 MPGR.
