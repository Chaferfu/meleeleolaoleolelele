   iBDW is a [19]Melee [20]smasher from Queens, New York, previously having attended college in Rochester, New York before graduating. He originally began competing out of curiosity for how far he could progress in late Spring of 2015, but after having difficulty finding practice in person he began grinding on Anther's Ladder, where he quickly rose to the top of the rankings. His play style makes heavy usage of [21]Fox's up-air for juggles, his tilts and mobility for zoning and approaching, and quick out of shield options to create openings or end stocks.

   He has wins over players such as [22]SFAT, [23]Swedish Delight, [24]HugS, [25]Westballz, [26]Shroomed, [27]lloD, [28]KJH, [29]Hax$, [30]Fiction, [31]ARMY, and many others. He is currently ranked 2nd on the [32]NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings and 34th on the [33]2018 MPGR.
