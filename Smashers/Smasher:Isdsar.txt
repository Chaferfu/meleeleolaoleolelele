   Isdsar is a [15]Melee [16]Captain Falcon player from Greece, currently living in Wales, ranked 1st on the [17]Greek Melee Power Rankings and 2nd on the [18]Welsh Melee Power Rankings, and is generally regarded as one of the best Falcon mains in Europe. He hosts and streams the Bi-Weekly tournament, "Inath's House", in Athens.
