   Jah Ridin' (pronounced: Djah) is a Melee Luigi player from Switzerland. His breakthrough performance was at LAST VI where he pulled an upset against Tekk and went on to secure 1st place. He is ranked 25th from April 2018 on the European Melee Power Rankings, 1st on the Swiss Melee Power Rankings, and 12th on the NorCal Melee Power Rankings. He has wins over other top players including Ralph, Kevbot, Gravy, Frenzy, SK92, Däumling, Eagle, and DjaGoF. He is part of the RomandyGaming team.
