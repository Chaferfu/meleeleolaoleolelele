   Jerry, also known as Obi and xMikeL, is a [17]Jigglypuff and [18]Fox main from Baltimore, Maryland. He is currently ranked 5th on the [19]MD/VA Melee Power Rankings and 55th on the [20]2018 MPGR. He has tournament wins over [21]SFAT, [22]ChuDat, [23]Swedish Delight, [24]dizzkidboogie, [25]Rishi, [26]lloD, [27]Captain Smuckers, [28]Ice, and [29]Milkman. He was also formerly ranked top 5 on SmashLadder.
