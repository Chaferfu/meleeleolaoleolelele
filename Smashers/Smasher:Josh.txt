   WormyNugget, also known as Josh, is an SSB4 Diddy Kong player from Miramar, Florida, considered one of the best Diddy Kong players from the state alongside MVD and dyr. He is currently ranked 3rd on the South Florida Power Rankings. He has taken sets off of players such as Pink Fresh, MVD and Manny.
