   K-12 is a professional Melee player from Madrid, Spain, who mains Donkey Kong, having formerly mained Samus. He is one of the two best Donkey Kong players in Europe, along with Moe. He has attended various tournaments outside of Spain, including Epita Smash Arena 3, where he placed 9th, and Super Lion 4, where he placed 17th. He is currently ranked 1st in Project M on the Madrid Power Rankings.
