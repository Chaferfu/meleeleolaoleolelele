   K-12 is a professional [21]Melee player from Madrid, Spain, who mains [22]Donkey Kong, having formerly mained [23]Samus. He is one of the two best Donkey Kong players in Europe, along with [24]Moe. He has attended various tournaments outside of Spain, including [25]Epita Smash Arena 3, where he placed 9th, and [26]Super Lion 4, where he placed 17th. He is currently ranked 1st in [27]Project M on the [28]Madrid Power Rankings.
