   KPAN is a [18]Melee [19]Falco main who has been playing competitively since 2012.

   Training with other students and top players at Georgia Tech, Kevin quickly grew to prominence to become one of the best Falco players in Georgia and the Southeast.

   In 2015, KPAN was a member of Georgia Tech's team for [20]The Melee Games, along with [21]Druggedfox, [22]Baka4moe, [23]Awstintacious, and [24]Bravo. After fighting their way to the finals they finished 2nd overall, losing to UC Irvine.

   He is currently ranked 3rd on the [25]Georgia Melee Power Rankings and 85th on the [26]2017 SSBMRank.
