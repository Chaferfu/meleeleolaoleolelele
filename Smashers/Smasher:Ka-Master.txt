   Ka-Master, also known as Luigi Ka-Master, is an American [21]smasher from the Washington area who was known in the 2007 era for his success in tournaments using [22]Luigi, and for his knowledge of the [23]space animal matchups. He has released two very well-received combo videos and is currently considered the best Luigi player in the world. He has taken sets off of players such as [24]Lucky, [25]Zhu, [26]HugS and [27]MacD.

   He also has a strong Captain Falcon, having taken sets off [28]Bladewise and [29]Iceman.

   Ka-Master is currently ranked 2nd on the [30]Washington Melee Power Rankings and 58th on the [31]2018 MPGR.
