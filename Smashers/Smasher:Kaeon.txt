   Kaeon is a Melee Fox main from New York. He is most well known for upsetting Nintendude in Round 1 pools at EVO 2016. He also took a set off Plup at Bad Moon Rising 2 while Plup was playing Falco. He is currently ranked 13th on the New York City Melee Power Rankings and 91st on the 2017 SSBMRank.
