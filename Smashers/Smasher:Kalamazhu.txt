   Kalamazhu is a Melee Peach main from Michigan who is currently residing in San Francisco, California. His highest placement at a super major was a 9th place performance at The Big House 4, where he beat players such as Lucky, VaNz, Porkchops, KirbyKaze, and MacD before losing to Hax in a close game 5. He also had a very impressive run at The Mango where he took a 2nd place finish, beating players such as Mango, SFAT, Syrox, CDK, and Fiction, before losing to Swedish Delight.

   Kalamazhu is currently ranked 9th on the NorCal Melee Power Rankings, 37th on the 2018 MPGR, and was formerly ranked 2nd on the Michigan Melee Power Rankings. He has shown expertise in the Samus matchup, beating players such as HugS and Darrell.
