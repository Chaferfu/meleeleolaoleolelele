   Kalamazhu is a [22]Melee [23]Peach main from Michigan who is currently residing in San Francisco, California. His highest placement at a super major was a 9th place performance at [24]The Big House 4, where he beat players such as [25]Lucky, [26]VaNz, [27]Porkchops, [28]KirbyKaze, and [29]MacD before losing to [30]Hax in a close game 5. He also had a very impressive run at [31]The Mango where he took a 2nd place finish, beating players such as [32]Mango, [33]SFAT, [34]Syrox, [35]CDK, and [36]Fiction, before losing to [37]Swedish Delight.

   Kalamazhu is currently ranked 9th on the [38]NorCal Melee Power Rankings, 37th on the [39]2018 MPGR, and was formerly ranked 2nd on the [40]Michigan Melee Power Rankings. He has shown expertise in the [41]Samus matchup, beating players such as [42]HugS and [43]Darrell.
