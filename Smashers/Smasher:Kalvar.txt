   Kalvar is a [16]smasher from New Hampshire who mains [17]Marth in [18]Melee. He is ranked 1st on the [19]New Hampshire Power Rankings, 3rd on the [20]New England Power Rankings, and 79th on the [21]2018 MPGR.
