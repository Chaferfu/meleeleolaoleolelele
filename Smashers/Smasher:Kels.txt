   Kels is a top Fox player with a strong Sheik secondary from Chicago, Illinois. He is currently ranked 1st on the Chicago Melee Power Rankings and 73rd on the 2018 MPGR. He is known as "the man, the myth, Kelly Smith" to his loyal fans. Kels is also noted to have upset Axe at The Big House 4 where he defeated him 3-1.
