   Kels is a top [31]Fox player with a strong [32]Sheik secondary from Chicago, Illinois. He is currently ranked 1st on the [33]Chicago Melee Power Rankings and 73rd on the [34]2018 MPGR. He is known as "the man, the myth, Kelly Smith" to his loyal fans. Kels is also noted to have upset [35]Axe at [36]The Big House 4 where he defeated him 3-1.
