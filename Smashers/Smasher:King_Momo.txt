      Real name     Michael Morales
       Location     Melbourne, FL [15] United States

   King Momo is a [16]Melee [17]Falco main currently ranked 6th on the [18]Central Florida Melee Power Rankings and 84th on the [19]2018 MPGR. He has taken tournament sets off players such as [20]The Moon, [21]Colbol, [22]DJ Nintendo and [23]Vro.
