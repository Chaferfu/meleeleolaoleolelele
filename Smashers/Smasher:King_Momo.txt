     Crew(s)    MVG League

       Ranking      Super Smash Bros. Melee 2018 MPGR: 84th
                          Additional info
      Real name     Michael Morales
       Location     Melbourne, FL  United States

   King Momo is a Melee Falco main currently ranked 6th on the Central Florida Melee Power Rankings and 84th on the 2018 MPGR. He has taken tournament sets off players such as The Moon, Colbol, DJ Nintendo and Vro.
