                               L "Alvin"
   Melee main Fox
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
       Ranking      Super Smash Bros. Melee 2017 SSBMRank: 94th
                            Additional info
      Real name     Alvin Nguyen
       Location     Northern California  United States

   L is a Melee Fox main from California who notably sent Wizzrobe to the losers bracket at The Big House 7. He is currently ranked 94th on the 2017 SSBMRank.
