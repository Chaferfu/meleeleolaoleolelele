   Legend is a [16]Melee [17]Jigglypuff main from Montreal, Quebec. He is considered to be the best Jigglypuff player in Canada. Legend caught the attention of the Smash scene after upsetting [18]KirbyKaze and easily beating [19]Bizzarro Flame at [20]Shine 2017. His win against Bizzarro Flame, while notable, is hardly surprising given Legend's matchup experience against [21]Ganondorf. The Jigglypuff player has played sets on a near weekly basis with [22]Kage, the best Ganondorf player in the world. Legend's other notable wins include [23]La Luna, [24]Nightmare, [25]Ryan Ford, [26]Dizzkidboogie, [27]Mafia, [28]Tian, and [29]Vwins. He is currently ranked 1st on the [30]Montreal Melee Power Rankings and 85th on the [31]2018 MPGR.
