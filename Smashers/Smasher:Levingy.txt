                           Levingy
   Melee mains Falco, Marth
         Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                       Additional info
       Location      Finland  Finland

   Levingy is a Finnish Melee Falco player who is considered to be the best player in Finland. He is currently ranked 31st on the European Melee Power Rankings. He most notably beat Bladewise and Overtriforce at EVO 2018.
