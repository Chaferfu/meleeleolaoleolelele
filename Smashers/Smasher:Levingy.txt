                           Levingy
   [6]Melee [7]mains [8]Falco, [9]Marth
         Skill       [10]Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                       Additional info
       Location      Finland [11] Finland

   Levingy is a Finnish [12]Melee [13]Falco player who is considered to be the best player in Finland. He is currently ranked 31st on the [14]European Melee Power Rankings. He most notably beat [15]Bladewise and [16]Overtriforce at [17]EVO 2018.
