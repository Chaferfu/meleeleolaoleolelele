   lloD is a [36]smasher from Great Falls, Virginia currently residing in McLean, Virginia who is a professional [37]Peach [38]main in all games in which she appears. He is [39]Rishi's younger brother. lloD is currently ranked 2nd on both the [40]MD/VA Melee and Project M Power Rankings, 11th on the [41]PMRank 2017, and 21st on the [42]2018 MPGR. He was formerly ranked 6th on the MD/VA Brawl Power Rankings and 10th on the MD/VA/DE Smash 4 Power Rankings.
