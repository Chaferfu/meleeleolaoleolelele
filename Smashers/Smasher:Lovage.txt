   Lovage is an American smasher who mainly uses Fox. In September 2011, he was ranked fourth on the SoCal Melee Power Rankings. He notably placed first in single and doubles, with S2J, at The Big House. He also placed 2nd at Northwest Manifest beating SFAT, Westballz, and Axe and took another set off of Westballz at Kings of Cali 3. He is currently ranked 78th on the 2017 SSBMRank.

Triviaedit

     * Lovage created one of the most well known tech skill videos, called Berserker.
          + Berserker was made by use of the Name Entry Glitch on Fixed Camera Melee.
     * He often directs his Twitter account to @S2JFALCON, S2J's account.
     * Lovage beat top players Bladewise and Leffen at the Utah Tournament GamingTyrant Expo at a time when he jokingly considered himself washed up.
