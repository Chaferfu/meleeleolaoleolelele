                         Lunar Dusk
   Melee main Ice Climbers
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                      Additional info
      Real name     Jean-Christophe Léger-Héroux
       Location     Longueuil, Québec  Canada

   Lunar Dusk is a Melee Ice Climbers main from Quebec. He is best known for taking a game off Leffen at LAN ETS 2017 (being the only person to do so at that tournament besides KirbyKaze). He is currently ranked 3rd on the Montreal Power Rankings.
