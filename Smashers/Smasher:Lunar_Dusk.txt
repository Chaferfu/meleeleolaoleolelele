                         Lunar Dusk
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Ice Climbers
        Skill       [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                      Additional info
      Real name     Jean-Christophe Léger-Héroux
       Location     Longueuil, Québec [10] Canada

   Lunar Dusk is a [11]Melee [12]Ice Climbers main from Quebec. He is best known for taking a game off [13]Leffen at [14]LAN ETS 2017 (being the only person to do so at that tournament besides [15]KirbyKaze). He is currently ranked 3rd on the [16]Montreal Power Rankings.
