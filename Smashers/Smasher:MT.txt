      Real name     Matthew Chan
       Location     Houston, Texas [11] USA

   MT is a smasher from Texas who mains [12]Marth in [13]Melee. He has had many notable placements in Texas tournaments. He is also a notable [14]tournament director, known for hosting the Forte tournament series in Austin, Texas. Currently unranked on the [15]MIOM Top 100, ranked 3rd on the [16]Central Texas Power Rankings, and ranked 1st on the Austin Power Rankings. He is a graduate of the University of Texas at Austin class of 2016.
