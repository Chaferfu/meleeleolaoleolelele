      Real name     Matthew Chan
       Location     Houston, Texas  USA

   MT is a smasher from Texas who mains Marth in Melee. He has had many notable placements in Texas tournaments. He is also a notable tournament director, known for hosting the Forte tournament series in Austin, Texas. Currently unranked on the MIOM Top 100, ranked 3rd on the Central Texas Power Rankings, and ranked 1st on the Austin Power Rankings. He is a graduate of the University of Texas at Austin class of 2016.
