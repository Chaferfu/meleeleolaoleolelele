   Makenshi is a Parisian smasher who mains [15]Marth and secondaries [16]Link. He became well-known in the French Smash community in 2004, when he attended RT in Dijon. As a fan of [17]Ken, he tried to improve, taking inspiration from the so-called "King of Smash", but in the end, he managed to develop his own style. He attended quite few European tournaments, and placed quite well, creating a rivalry with Swedish Marth player [18]Ek. He is currently ranked 8th on the French Melee Power Rankings and 34th on the [19]European Melee Power Rankings. He is one of the best Marth mains in Europe.

   When [20]Brawl was released in the US, he declared he would quit [21]Melee, but came back to it after a few months, saying that Brawl was too limited for him.
