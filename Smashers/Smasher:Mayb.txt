   mayb is a [16]SSBM [17]Peach and [18]Jigglypuff player originally from West New York, New Jersey and as of April 2018, Daytona Beach, FL, who joined the Melee community first on [19]Dolphin in late 2015 but started entering offline tournaments in early 2016. He is a rising star in the New Jersey and NYC melee community, having taken sets from [20]ARMY, [21]Hax (On the B0xx), [22]Captain Smuckers, [23]Slox, [24]2Saint, [25]Abate, [26]Squid, [27]Drunksloth, [28]King Momo, [29]Zealous5000, and many other players in the, NYC, NJ, and Florida SSBM scenes. He was formerly ranked 1st on the [30]New Jersey Power Rankings and 11th on the [31]NYC Metropolitan Power Rankings.
