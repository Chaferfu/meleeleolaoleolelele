   mayb is a SSBM Peach and Jigglypuff player originally from West New York, New Jersey and as of April 2018, Daytona Beach, FL, who joined the Melee community first on Dolphin in late 2015 but started entering offline tournaments in early 2016. He is a rising star in the New Jersey and NYC melee community, having taken sets from ARMY, Hax (On the B0xx), Captain Smuckers, Slox, 2Saint, Abate, Squid, Drunksloth, King Momo, Zealous5000, and many other players in the, NYC, NJ, and Florida SSBM scenes. He was formerly ranked 1st on the New Jersey Power Rankings and 11th on the NYC Metropolitan Power Rankings.
