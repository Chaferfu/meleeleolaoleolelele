   Uncle Mojo, also known as simply Mojo, is a smasher from Texas who mains [29]Fox in [30]Melee. He had been widely considered the #1 player in Texas since mid-2016 until he was outranked by Bananas in 2018. He is currently ranked 2nd on the [31]Central Texas Melee Power Rankings, 4th on the Houston Project M Power Rankings, and 80th on the [32]2018 MPGR.
