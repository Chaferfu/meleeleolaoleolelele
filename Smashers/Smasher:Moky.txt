   Moky is a Melee Fox main from Ontario, Canada and is an up and coming player within the Toronto scene. He is becoming an increasingly feared and well recognized opponent in the Greater Toronto area smash scene. Moky is ranked 2nd on the Ontario Melee Power Rankings and 52nd on the 2018 MPGR. He notably beat ChuDat at GENESIS 5 and Ryan Ford at DreamHack Montreal 2018.
