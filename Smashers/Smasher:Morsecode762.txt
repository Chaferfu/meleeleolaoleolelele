   Morsecode762 is a [19]smasher from Michigan who mains [20]Samus in [21]Melee and [22]Project M. He is considered to be the best [23]Samus player in the post dev shutdown era of PM, having placed 7th at PM in the Big House 2 and 2nd at [24]Frozen Phoenix 2017, with wins over players like [25]Sylarius, [26]Dirtboy, [27]Ripple, [28]XYK, and [29]lloD. In Melee, he has wins over players such as [30]Anther, [31]Fork and [32]Sauc3. He is currently ranked 3rd on the [33]Michigan Project M Power Rankings, 4th on the Michigan Melee Power Rankings, 21st on the [34]PMRank 2018, and 99th on the [35]2018 MPGR.
