   MrLz (also known as 4est) is a [28]smasher from Florida who is considered to be the best [29]Project M [30]Mr. Game & Watch [31]main in the world. He is known for performing extremely well despite his young age. He won the second largest Project M tournament of all time, [32]Paragon Los Angeles 2015 at only 13 years of age.

   In April of 2017, he suffered from an aortic dissection, and received successful emergency surgery. [33]Hungrybox created a [34]GoFundMe page to cover the fees of the surgery, reaching the goal of $3,500 in less than a day.
