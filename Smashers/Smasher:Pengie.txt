                           Pengie
   Melee main Fox
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                      Additional info
      Real name     Juan Cobian
       Location     Florida  United States of America

   Pengie is a Melee Fox main from Florida. He has beaten such players as Gahtzu and Drunksloth. He was previously ranked 7th on the Central Florida Power Rankings.
