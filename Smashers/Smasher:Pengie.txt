                           Pengie
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Fox
        Skill       [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                      Additional info
      Real name     Juan Cobian
       Location     Florida [10] United States of America

   Pengie is a [11]Melee [12]Fox main from Florida. He has beaten such players as [13]Gahtzu and [14]Drunksloth. He was previously ranked 7th on the [15]Central Florida Power Rankings.
