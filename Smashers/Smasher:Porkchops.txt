   Porkchops is a Melee Falco player from Florida. Since 2014, he has been making an effort to go to plenty of out of state tournaments such as Apex 2014, SKTAR 3, and MLG Anaheim 2014. He is currently ranked 1st on the South Florida Melee Power Rankings and was formerly ranked 2nd on the South Florida Project M Power Rankings.

Tournament historyedit

Super Smash Bros. Meleeedit

   Porkchops had been an avid Melee player for years, but had little national presence until 2014. He had a greatly improved performance in 2014, including a clutch set against Smash Studios's Uuaa at CEO 2014 Prologue and an upset against aMSa at CEO 2014.

Project Medit

   In Project M he is by far most well known for his victory over K9 at SKTAR 3.
