   Qerb is a Mr. Game & Watch player from New Jersey. He is widely considered to be the best Mr. Game & Watch player in the world, most notably taking a set off La Luna's Marth (considered to be one of his worst matchups) resulting in a huge upset, He also took a game off Hungrybox. He is currently ranked 13th on the Tristate Melee Power Rankings and 6th on the New Jersey Melee Power Rankings.
