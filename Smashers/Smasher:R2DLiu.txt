   R2DLiu is a [16]smasher from Delaware, United States who mains [17]Fox. He is ranked 2nd on the [18]Philadelphia Power Rankings and 2nd on the [19]Delaware Power Rankings. He became well known for getting an unexpected 33rd at [20]The Big House 6 upsetting [21]Axe in winners before losing a very close 2-1 set to [22]KirbyKaze as well as a set to [23]Ryan Ford. He also has wins over the likes of [24]The Moon, [25]Mafia, [26]Lovage, and [27]JFlex.
