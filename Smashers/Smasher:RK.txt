   RK (also known as :eggplantthink:, LIT$TAR and SEKHU) is a Canadian [10]smasher from British Columbia. He is currently ranked 2nd on the [11]B.C. Canada Smash Power Rankings. He notably defeated [12]Wizzrobe in round one pools at [13]Don't Park on the Grass. He also placed in the top 64 at [14]GENESIS 4 defeating [15]Pengie, [16]DairDev1L, and [17]Squid. He also has wins over players in the Northwest, such as [18]Iceman, [19]Rustin, [20]FatGoku, and [21]Ka-Master.
