   RK (also known as :eggplantthink:, LIT$TAR and SEKHU) is a Canadian smasher from British Columbia. He is currently ranked 2nd on the B.C. Canada Smash Power Rankings. He notably defeated Wizzrobe in round one pools at Don't Park on the Grass. He also placed in the top 64 at GENESIS 4 defeating Pengie, DairDev1L, and Squid. He also has wins over players in the Northwest, such as Iceman, Rustin, FatGoku, and Ka-Master.
