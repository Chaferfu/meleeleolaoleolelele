   Ralph is a Melee Falco and Fox main from Northern California who is the anchor for the unexpected winning UC Berkeley team in The Melee Games. He is currently ranked 8th on the NorCal Melee Power Rankings and 89th on the 2018 MPGR. He attended Super Nebulous 3 where along with winning The Melee Games, he got 9th place. He has wins over Captain Faceroll, Toph, s0ft, Kira, Shroomed, Laudandus, MattDotZeb, Azusa, and Westballz.
