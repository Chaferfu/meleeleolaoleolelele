   Ralph is a [22]Melee [23]Falco and [24]Fox [25]main from Northern California who is the anchor for the unexpected winning UC Berkeley team in [26]The Melee Games. He is currently ranked 8th on the [27]NorCal Melee Power Rankings and 89th on the [28]2018 MPGR. He attended [29]Super Nebulous 3 where along with winning The Melee Games, he got 9th place. He has wins over [30]Captain Faceroll, [31]Toph, [32]s0ft, [33]Kira, [34]Shroomed, [35]Laudandus, [36]MattDotZeb, [37]Azusa, and [38]Westballz.
