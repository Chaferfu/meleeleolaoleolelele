      Real name     Austin Reed
       Location     Louisville, Kentucky  United States

   Reeve is a smasher from Kentucky who mains Marth in Melee. He notably defeated SFAT 2-0 in pools at Eden and also has wins over Azusa, Bobby Frizz, Android and 4%. He is currently ranked 1st on the Melee Kentucky Power Rankings and 101st on the 2017 SSBMRank.
