   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Marth
      [9]Crew(s)    Spire
   Former [10]crews OP
        Skill       [11]Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
       Ranking      [12]Super Smash Bros. Melee [13]2017 SSBMRank: 101st
                             Additional info
      Real name     Austin Reed
       Location     Louisville, Kentucky [14] United States

   Reeve is a smasher from Kentucky who mains [15]Marth in [16]Melee. He notably defeated [17]SFAT 2-0 in pools at [18]Eden and also has wins over [19]Azusa, [20]Bobby Frizz, [21]Android and [22]4%. He is currently ranked 1st on the Melee [23]Kentucky Power Rankings and 101st on the [24]2017 SSBMRank.
