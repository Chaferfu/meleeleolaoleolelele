                                [6]Rik
   [7]Melee [8]mains [9]Fox, [10]Falco
         Skill       [11]Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
        Ranking      [12]Super Smash Bros. Melee [13]2018 MPGR: 82nd
                           Additional info
       Real name     Erik Gerlitz
       Location      Zionsville, Indiana [14] United States

   Rik is an [15]Melee [16]Fox and [17]Falco main currently ranked 1st on the [18]Indiana Melee Power Rankings and 82nd on the [19]2018 MPGR. He has wins over players such as [20]Westballz, [21]Rishi, [22]KJH, [23]Kels, [24]Drephen, [25]Captain Faceroll, [26]Redd, [27]Tai, and [28]Kage.
