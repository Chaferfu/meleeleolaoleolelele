                                 Rik

   Melee mains Fox, Falco
         Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
        Ranking      Super Smash Bros. Melee 2018 MPGR: 82nd
                           Additional info
       Real name     Erik Gerlitz
       Location      Zionsville, Indiana  United States

   Rik is an Melee Fox and Falco main currently ranked 1st on the Indiana Melee Power Rankings and 82nd on the 2018 MPGR. He has wins over players such as Westballz, Rishi, KJH, Kels, Drephen, Captain Faceroll, Redd, Tai, and Kage.
