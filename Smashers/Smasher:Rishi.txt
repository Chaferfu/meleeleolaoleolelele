   Rishi, formerly known as SmashG0D, is a smasher from Virginia currently residing in New York City who is known for his proficiency in multiple Smash games, maining Fox in Smash 64, Marth in Melee, Samus in Brawl, Mewtwo and Roy in Project M, and Kirby in Smash 4.

   In Melee, he has wins over top players such as Mew2King, ChuDat, Swedish Delight, HugS, Westballz, Nintendude, Zain, lloD, Colbol, Professor Pro, and Slox. At EVO 2016, he took a game off of Armada but lost the set in a last stock last hit situation. He is currently ranked 1st on the NYC Metropolitan Area Power Rankings, 26th on the 2018 MPGR, and was formerly ranked 2nd on the MD/VA Melee Power Rankings.

   In Smash 4, Rishi notably beat False and has taken games off of top players such as Ranai and Larry Lurr. He was formerly ranked 5th on the MD/VA Smash 4 Power Rankings.

   He was formerly ranked 3rd on the MD/VA Project M Power Rankings.

   He is lloD's older brother; they frequently team in many Smash games. They notably placed 5th at The Big House 7, 5th at Smash 'N' Splash 4, 3rd at Get On My Level 2018, and 5th at Super Smash Con 2015 in both Melee and Smash 4.
