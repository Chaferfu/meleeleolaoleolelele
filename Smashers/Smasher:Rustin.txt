                                  Rustin
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Peach
        Skill       [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee Top Professional (retired)
                             Additional info
      Real name     Rustin Winger
       Location     Washington [10] United States

   Rustin is a [11]Melee [12]Peach main from Washington. He has beaten players such as [13]Captain Faceroll, [14]Squid, [15]Ka-Master, [16]FatGoku, and [17]KPAN. He was ranked as high as 4th on the [18]Washington Power Rankings.
