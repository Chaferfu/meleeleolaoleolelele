      Real name     Rustin Winger
       Location     Washington  United States

   Rustin is a Melee Peach main from Washington. He has beaten players such as Captain Faceroll, Squid, Ka-Master, FatGoku, and KPAN. He was ranked as high as 4th on the Washington Power Rankings.
