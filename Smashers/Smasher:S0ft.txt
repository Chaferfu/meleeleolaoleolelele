   s0ft is a Melee Jigglypuff main from Georgia, who is considered one of the best Jigglypuff players in the United States, behind Hungrybox and Prince Abu. He plays an unorthodox, grounded version of Jigglypuff. He finished 7th at Apex 2014, defeating many top players and beating Ice twice in the second round of bracket pools. s0ft qualified for the MLG Anaheim 2014 championship bracket on points from strong placements in the qualifiers CEO 2014 Prologue, Fighters' Edge, WHOBO MLG, Get On My Level, and SKTAR 3. He was formerly ranked 82nd on the 2015 SSBMRank and 2nd on the Georgia Melee Power Rankings, and is currently ranked 13th on the NorCal Melee Power Rankings.
