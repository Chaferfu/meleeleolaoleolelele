   s0ft is a [29]Melee [30]Jigglypuff [31]main from Georgia, who is considered one of the best Jigglypuff players in the United States, behind [32]Hungrybox and [33]Prince Abu. He plays an unorthodox, grounded version of Jigglypuff. He finished 7th at [34]Apex 2014, defeating many top players and beating [35]Ice twice in the second round of bracket pools. s0ft qualified for the [36]MLG Anaheim 2014 championship bracket on points from strong placements in the qualifiers [37]CEO 2014 Prologue, [38]Fighters' Edge, [39]WHOBO MLG, [40]Get On My Level, and [41]SKTAR 3. He was formerly ranked 82nd on the [42]2015 SSBMRank and 2nd on the [43]Georgia Melee Power Rankings, and is currently ranked 13th on the [44]NorCal Melee Power Rankings.
