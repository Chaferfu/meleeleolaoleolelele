   Santiago, also known as Santi, and formerly known as Lil Fumi, is a Melee smasher from San Diego, California. He is currently ranked 1st on the San Diego Melee Power Rankings, 10th on the SoCal Melee Power Rankings, and 48th on the 2018 MPGR. He was originally known as a Yoshi main and has gone through several characters including Fox, Marth, Young Link, and Sheik. He now mains Falco and does not plan to switch again. Santiago is a Math Major at UCI and helped UCI become national Melee champions at The Melee Games.
