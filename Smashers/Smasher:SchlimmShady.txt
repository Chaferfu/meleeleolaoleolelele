     Melee mains    Mario, Dr. Mario

        Crew(s)       SFS
           Skill          Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                          Additional info
         Real name        Faed Shadid
          Location        Germany  Germany

   SchlimmShady is a Dr. Mario main from Germany who is considered to be the best Mario player in Europe. He is currently ranked 7th on the German Melee Power Rankings and 44th on the European Melee Power Rankings. He has notably beaten Overtriforce.
