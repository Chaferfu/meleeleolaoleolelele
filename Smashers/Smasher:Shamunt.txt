                           Shamunt
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Sheik
      Sponsor(s)    [9]Trail Gaming League
        Skill       [10]Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                       Additional info
      Real name     Tommy Tiger
       Location     South Florida [11] USA

   Shamunt is a South Florida [12]Sheik main who is currently ranked 4th in South Florida for Melee. He is sponsored by [13]Trail Gaming League, a sponsor he created himself. He has wins over [14]Harriettheguy, [15]A Rookie, [16]Green Mario, and [17]MrLz
