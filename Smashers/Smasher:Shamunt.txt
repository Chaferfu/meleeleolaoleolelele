                           Shamunt
   Melee main Sheik
      Sponsor(s)    Trail Gaming League
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                       Additional info
      Real name     Tommy Tiger
       Location     South Florida  USA

   Shamunt is a South Florida Sheik main who is currently ranked 4th in South Florida for Melee. He is sponsored by Trail Gaming League, a sponsor he created himself. He has wins over Harriettheguy, A Rookie, Green Mario, and MrLz
