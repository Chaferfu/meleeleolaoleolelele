   Sharkz is a Smasher from North Carolina who mains Ice Climbers in Melee and Ice Climbers and Pit in Project M. He is currently considered to be the third best Project M Ice Climbers player in the world, behind techboy and Phresh, especially given his 2nd place at Project M in The Big House. There, he defeated XYK, Professor Pro, Dirtboy, and Anther to make it to Grand Finals, only losing to Frozen in two sets. He also has wins over players like ESAM, BaconPancakes, Mr. R and Hyperflame. He often teams with Gato in both Project M and Melee, and they are certainly a solid team, having defeated Hungrybox and Mew2King in 2 sets of Grand Finals in Project M at WTFox 2. He has wins over Zain, Colbol, Rishi, Ginger, Captain Smuckers, Gravy, Drephen and iBDW in Melee. He is currently ranked 1st and 4th on the North Carolina Melee and Project M Power Rankings (and is currently
   considered the best Project M player in North Carolina), respectively, and 70th on the 2018 MPGR.
