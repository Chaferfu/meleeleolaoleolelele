   Sharkz is a [24]Smasher from North Carolina who mains [25]Ice Climbers in [26]Melee and Ice Climbers and [27]Pit in [28]Project M. He is currently considered to be the third best Project M Ice Climbers player in the world, behind [29]techboy and [30]Phresh, especially given his 2nd place at Project M in The Big House. There, he defeated [31]XYK, [32]Professor Pro, [33]Dirtboy, and [34]Anther to make it to Grand Finals, only losing to [35]Frozen in two sets. He also has wins over players like [36]ESAM, [37]BaconPancakes, [38]Mr. R and [39]Hyperflame. He often teams with [40]Gato in both Project M and Melee, and they are certainly a solid team, having defeated [41]Hungrybox and [42]Mew2King in 2 sets of Grand Finals in Project M at [43]WTFox 2. He has wins over [44]Zain, [45]Colbol, [46]Rishi, [47]Ginger, [48]Captain Smuckers, [49]Gravy, [50]Drephen and [51]iBDW in Melee. He is currently ranked 1st and 4th on the [52]North Carolina Melee and Project M Power Rankings (and is currently
   considered the best Project M player in North Carolina), respectively, and 70th on the [53]2018 MPGR.
