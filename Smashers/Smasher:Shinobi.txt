                          Shinobi
   Melee main Fox
                      Additional info
      Real name     Austin
       Location     Columbia, Missouri  United States

   Shinobi is a Melee Fox main from Kansas City. He is currently ranked 1st on Missouri Power Rankings and was listed as a "hidden boss" on the 2015 SSBMRank.
