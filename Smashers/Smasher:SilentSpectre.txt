   SilentSpectre is an [22]SSBM [23]Captain Falcon player from Northern California, famed for his extremely flashy style of play, extensive combo game titular of his character, and creative and unorthodox usages of Falcon's [24]Knee Smash and [25]moonwalk. In his prime, SilentSpectre was considered to be the best Captain Falcon on the West Coast, and one of the best in the country. He is currently ranked 19th on the [26]NorCal Melee Power Rankings, and was formerly ranked 5th on the [27]NorCal Smash 4 Power Rankings.

   At [28]Pound 4, SilentSpectre teamed with fellow Falcon player, [29]Darkrain, and placed 2nd, an unexpectedly high placing given the team composition. In Singles, he upset [30]Armada, and placed 9th.

Trivia[[146]edit]

     * SilentSpectre often teams with [147]Tang, with whom he performed the famous [148]Wombo Combo.
     * SilentSpectre often chooses the green palette swap, or green team for [149]Captain Falcon, as well as the fourth controller port, which also has a green background.
     * SilentSpectre is one of two players to have a positive record against [150]Armada (1-0), the other being [151]Captain Jack (2-0).
