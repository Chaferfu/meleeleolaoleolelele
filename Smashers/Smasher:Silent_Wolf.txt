   Silent Wolf is an American [32]smasher located in the Washington area. He has defeated top players such as [33]Mew2King, [34]Leffen, and [35]PPMD. He was ranked 20th on the [36]2016 SSBMRank.

   At [37]EVO 2014, Silent Wolf placed 7th in Melee singles. Although he lost to [38]Darkrain 0-2 early in winners' bracket in the winners' quarters pool, he made a very long run through the losers' bracket, defeating players such as [39]Cactuar and [40]Duck to make it into the top 32, and then defeating [41]SFAT 2-1, [42]Shroomed, [43]Fiction 2-0, and Leffen 2-1. In losers' top 8, he was eliminated by [44]Axe 1-2, in addition to being four stocked in one of the matches that took only less than a minute.

   At [45]HTC Throwdown, Silent Wolf easily cruised into top 8 in winners side, beating Ralph, Shroomed and [46]DruggedFox along the way. In top 8 he lost to Leffen 0-3 right away. In losers he beat Mew2King and DruggedFox again. In losers finals he won game 1 against [47]Hungrybox, but ended up losing to him 1-3.

   He is also one of the best Donkey Kong Country speedrunners.

   On January 15th, 2018, Silent Wolf announced his retirement from competitive play, citing a lack of interest.^[48][1]

Playstyle[[58]edit]

   Silent Wolf is one of the premier [59]Fox mains in the world, renowned for his extremely technical playstyle and flashy combos. He is proficient in the [60]Peach matchup consistently beating [61]Bladewise at his locals, [62]MacD at a number of events, and [63]Mafia at [64]The Big House 6. He even took a game off of [65]Armada at [66]EVO 2015.
