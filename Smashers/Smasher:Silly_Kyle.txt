   Silly Kyle is a [20]Peach player from Arizona. He is currently ranked 3rd on the [21]Arizona Melee Power Rankings. He notably beat [22]Axe's Pikachu at a [23]tourney in Mesa. He was previously ranked 1st on the Arizona Project M Power Rankings, and still is one of the best Peach players in [24]Project M.
