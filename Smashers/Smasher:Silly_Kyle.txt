   Silly Kyle is a Peach player from Arizona. He is currently ranked 3rd on the Arizona Melee Power Rankings. He notably beat Axe's Pikachu at a tourney in Mesa. He was previously ranked 1st on the Arizona Project M Power Rankings, and still is one of the best Peach players in Project M.
