   Slox is an American [21]Melee [22]smasher who started playing competitively in Connecticut around 2009, known for his proficiency with multiple characters such as [23]Fox, [24]Sheik, [25]Captain Falcon, and [26]Marth. He is currently ranked 2nd on the [27]New England Melee Power Rankings, 6th on the [28]NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings, and 50th on the [29]2018 MPGR.
