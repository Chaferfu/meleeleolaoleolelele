   Spark is a Sheik main on the comeup who made a name for himself through netplay. He is currently ranked 4th on the NorCal Melee Power Rankings, 10th on the NorCal Project M Power Rankings, and 53rd on the 2018 MPGR. He has taken sets off of Westballz, Shroomed, dizzkidboogie, Nintendude, Laudandus, Captain Smuckers, and Overtriforce.
