   Stango, also referred to as Stahngo and formerly known as Lawn Chair, is a Melee and Project M player from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania who is currently residing in Delaware. He primarily uses Marth with Fox as a secondary. His most notable placing thus far has been at GENESIS 5 where he defeated strong players such as Colbol, Android, Darkatma and Abate as well as taking Mango and Crush to game 5 before finishing at 17th. He also has mutiple wins over Mafia most famously in Apex 2016 where he made winners top 8. He is currently ranked 1st on the Delaware Melee Power Rankings and 62nd on the 2018 MPGR.
