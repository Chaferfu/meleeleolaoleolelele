                           Sverre
   Melee main Captain Falcon
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                      Additional info
      Real name     Sverre Vinje
       Location     Bærum, Norway  Norway

   Sverre is a Norwegian Melee Captain Falcon main. He is ranked 2nd on the Norwegian Power Rankings and 27th on the European Melee Power Rankings. He has wins over players such as Zorc, Kira, and Daydee, as well as a close set with Overtriforce at Valhalla.
