                           Sverre
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Captain Falcon
        Skill       [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                      Additional info
      Real name     Sverre Vinje
       Location     Bærum, Norway [10] Norway

   Sverre is a Norwegian [11]Melee [12]Captain Falcon main. He is ranked 2nd on the [13]Norwegian Power Rankings and 27th on the [14]European Melee Power Rankings. He has wins over players such as [15]Zorc, [16]Kira, and [17]Daydee, as well as a close set with [18]Overtriforce at [19]Valhalla.
