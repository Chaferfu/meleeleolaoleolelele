   SwiftBass, also known as Swift, GibsonZero, and Phantom Renegade, is an American Melee Marth main who started playing competitively in Pennsylvania around 2005. He played in his hometown in Connecticut before moving to Pittsburgh for college. Now he lives in Connecticut and trains with the likes of Slox and Lord HDL. SwiftBass is currently ranked 14th on the New England Melee Power Rankings. He was formerly ranked 86th on the 2015 SSBMRank and is currently ranked 19th on the NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings.

Biographyedit

   A long-time veteran of the Melee community, SwiftBass has seen his fair share of tournaments. While criss-crossing the east coast, he's picked up remarkable experience and a patient, consistent Marth and impressive bracket placements in 2015 — including placings in the final brackets of Apex 2015 and The Big House 5.
