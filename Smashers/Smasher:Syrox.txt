   Syrox is a Melee Fox main from Colorado who is currently ranked 1st on the Colorado Melee Power Rankings and 36th on the 2018 MPGR. He was picked up by eSports organization Balance Gaming on June 17th, 2017.^

   In early 2016, Syrox attended GENESIS 3 and finished 65th after beating Animal, Aza and Hamyojo. He took a game from PewPewU before being eliminated by Chillin.

   He had a remarkable performance at Rewired 2016, beating Gahtzu and Forward and taking Axe to game 5 before finishing at 3rd place.

   In December 2016, Syrox finished the year strong with another amazing run at Eden, taking sets off DJ Nintendo, Ginger, Colbol, and S2J before losing to Ice in losers quarters 2 - 3.

   He has other notable wins against SFAT (at ASH@WIT #88), Westballz (at SSS #55), Shroomed (at DreamHack Austin 2017) n0ne (at SSS #55), Professor Pro, Lucky, and more.

   He has also taken a game off of Hungrybox at Smash Conference LXIX.
