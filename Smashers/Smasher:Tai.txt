   Tai is a professional [19]Melee [20]Marth player from Phoenix, Arizona. He is renowned for his exceptional [21]tech skill and creative punish game with Marth, and is considered one of the better Marth players on the West Coast. Tai is currently ranked 3rd on the [22]Arizona Melee Power Rankings and 72nd on the [23]2018 MPGR.
