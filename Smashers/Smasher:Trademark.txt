   Trademark is a Peach, Sheik, Marth, and Fox quad main from New Haven, Connecticut. He also uses Mario and Yoshi in matches as well. He was once ranked as high as 4th on the Connecticut Melee Power Rankings. He now lives in Texas and has attended Texas majors. In between his life in Connecticut and Texas, he lived in Florida for a little bit. He has wins over G$, Watty, Ridz, Mogwai, Lad, and SPAWN.
