   Trif, or Trifasia, is a Melee Peach player from Spain. Despite only having gone international in 2014, he quickly became known for his solid play at his first national tournament, B.E.A.S.T 4, where he placed 9th. He has also pulled notable upsets over Abate at B.E.A.S.T 6, Druggedfox at Don't Park on the Grass, Professor Pro at Smashbox 2, and MacD at DreamHack Winter 2016. During the second half of 2017 he established himself as being a contender for the 3rd best in Europe, with consistent victories against Professor Pro, Amsah, and a positive record over Ice. His other notable wins include Swedish Delight, KirbyKaze, Redd, AbsentPage, Junebug, and iBDW. He is currently ranked 1st on the Spanish Melee Power Rankings, 3rd on the European Melee Power Rankings, and 35th on the 2018 MPGR. He is also currently ranked 2nd on the Madrid Project M Power Rankings.
