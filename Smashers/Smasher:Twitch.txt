        Skill       [10]Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
                         Additional info
      Real name     Alex Nanney
       Location     North Carolina [11] United States

   Twitch is a professional [12]smasher from North Carolina who mains [13]Fox. Twitch had for a while been a high-ranking player on the [14]North Carolina Melee Power Rankings, last ranking 2nd on the Summer 2016 list, and is also [15]PPMD's younger brother. His most notable tournament placing was 3rd at Bad Moon Rising, a regional in North Carolina, where he defeated [16]Colbol, who was then ranked third in the [17]Florida Power Rankings, twice, as well as defeating [18]DruggedFox. Twitch finished behind [19]Hungrybox and [20]Plup, but ahead of players such as [21]Nintendude and [22]Wizzrobe. In the same tournament he teamed with [23]$mike and finished fifth. Other victories by Twitch prior to Bad Moon Rising include [24]Arc, [25]ESAM, [26]baka4moe, and [27]Mike Haze.
