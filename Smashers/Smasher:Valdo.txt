                              Valdo
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Fox
      [9]Crew(s)    Zaga Talent Gaming
        Skill       [10]Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
                         Additional info
      Real name     César Osvaldo Cantú
      Birth date    April 30, 1989 (age 29)
       Location     Monterrey [11] Mexico

   Valdo is a smasher from Monterrey, Mexico who mains [12]Fox in [13]Melee and is sponsored by Zaga Talent Gaming. He's known as one of the best players from Monterrey along [14]JAVI, [15]Aza and [16]Twin. He is also considered one of the top Mexican smashers. He is the best [17]Fox in Mexico after Javi, boasting wins over players like [18]ZeRo, [19]Eddy Mexico, [20]Medz, [21]Aza, [22]MT and [23]Albert.
