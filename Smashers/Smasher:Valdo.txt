                              Valdo
   Melee main Fox
      Crew(s)    Zaga Talent Gaming
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Top professional
                         Additional info
      Real name     César Osvaldo Cantú
      Birth date    April 30, 1989 (age 29)
       Location     Monterrey  Mexico

   Valdo is a smasher from Monterrey, Mexico who mains Fox in Melee and is sponsored by Zaga Talent Gaming. He's known as one of the best players from Monterrey along JAVI, Aza and Twin. He is also considered one of the top Mexican smashers. He is the best Fox in Mexico after Javi, boasting wins over players like ZeRo, Eddy Mexico, Medz, Aza, MT and Albert.
