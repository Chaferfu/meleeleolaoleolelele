                          Additional info
         Real name        Vish Rajkumar
          Location        Lake City, Washington  USA

   Vish is an SSBM Captain Falcon main from Washington, though is more recognised for his commentary, and frequently commentates with Gimpyfish at Washington tournaments. He was formerly ranked 84th on the 2013 SSBM Rankings.
