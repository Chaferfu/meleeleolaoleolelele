   vortex is a [19]Melee and [20]Project M player who resides in Westchester, New York and attends Stony Brook University. Long considered a hidden boss in the New York Scene, he began travelling consistently, picking up wins over [21]satdaddy at [22]GENESIS 4 along with [23]Ryobeat and [24]King Momo at [25]Super Smash Con 2017, then subsequently breaking out at [26]The Big House 7 with his win over [27]Westballz and a top 64 placing. In 2018, he has picked up wins over [28]Ginger, [29]Medz, and [30]Ralph at various majors. He is currently ranked 10th on the [31]NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings and 96th on the [32]2018 MPGR.
