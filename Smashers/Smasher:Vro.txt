   Vro is a smasher from Chicago, Illinois who mains [25]Marth in [26]Melee and [27]Wario in [28]Project M. He placed 2nd in Project M singles at [29]MELEE-FC10R Legacy. He had a very close set with [30]Hax at [31]The Big House 4 which went down to the last stock, taking the first game before losing two close games after. He is ranked 104th on the [32]2017 SSBMRank.
