   Vro is a smasher from Chicago, Illinois who mains Marth in Melee and Wario in Project M. He placed 2nd in Project M singles at MELEE-FC10R Legacy. He had a very close set with Hax at The Big House 4 which went down to the last stock, taking the first game before losing two close games after. He is ranked 104th on the 2017 SSBMRank.
