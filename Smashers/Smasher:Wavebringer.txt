                         Wavebringer
                        Wavebringer
   Melee main Peach
     Crew(s)    The Winning Clan
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                       Additional info
      Real name     Jay Salandanan
       Location     Berkeley, California  USA

   Wavebringer is a NorCal Peach main who is currently ranked 4th in Berkeley. He is considered to have one of the best Peachs in NorCal, along with Azusa, Kalamazhu, and Darkatma. He has wins over Ralph, MattDotZeb, s0ft, Berble, Cereal Rabbit, Alan, and ARMY.
