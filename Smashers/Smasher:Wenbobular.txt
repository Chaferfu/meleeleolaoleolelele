   Wenbobular, also known as Wenbo, is a [15]Fox main from Maryland, with strong [16]Falco and [17]Captain Falcon secondaries. He was formerly ranked 15th on the [18]MD/VA Melee Power Rankings. He has tournament wins over [19]aMSa and [20]Chillin among others.
