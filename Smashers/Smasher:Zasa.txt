                            Zasa
   Melee main Jigglypuff
        Skill       Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                      Additional info
      Real name     William Matson
       Location     North Carolina  United States

   Zasa is a Melee Jigglypuff main from North Carolina. He is currently ranked 5th on the North Carolina Melee Power Rankings. He made a deep run through losers at Function(1), defeating Yort, R2DLiu, and KPAN before losing to Colbol. He then notably knocked Colbol out of winners at Bad Moon Rising 2, and took 9th out of a field of 369. He has also regularly taken sets off top local players such as $mike, Ronzell, and Sharkz.
