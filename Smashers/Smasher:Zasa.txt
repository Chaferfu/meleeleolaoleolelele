                            Zasa
   [6]Melee [7]main [8]Jigglypuff
        Skill       [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee Professional
                      Additional info
      Real name     William Matson
       Location     North Carolina [10] United States

   Zasa is a [11]Melee [12]Jigglypuff main from North Carolina. He is currently ranked 5th on the [13]North Carolina Melee Power Rankings. He made a deep run through losers at [14]Function(1), defeating [15]Yort, [16]R2DLiu, and [17]KPAN before losing to [18]Colbol. He then notably knocked [19]Colbol out of winners at [20]Bad Moon Rising 2, and took 9th out of a field of 369. He has also regularly taken sets off top local players such as [21]$mike, [22]Ronzell, and [23]Sharkz.
