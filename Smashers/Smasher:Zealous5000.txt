   Zealous5000, is a Marth main from New Jersey who is currently ranked 2nd on the New Jersey Power Rankings, 15th on the NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings, and 98th on the 2017 SSBMRank. He has wins over MacD, Stango, R2DLiu, Animal, Green Ranger, dudutsai, Wobbles, and Alex19.
