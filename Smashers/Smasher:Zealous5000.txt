   Zealous5000, is a Marth main from New Jersey who is currently ranked 2nd on the New Jersey Power Rankings, 15th on the [16]NYC Metropolitan Area Melee Power Rankings, and 98th on the [17]2017 SSBMRank. He has wins over [18]MacD, [19]Stango, [20]R2DLiu, [21]Animal, [22]Green Ranger, [23]dudutsai, [24]Wobbles, and [25]Alex19.
