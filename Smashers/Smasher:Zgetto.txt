   Zgetto, also known as Miguel, is a Fox player from Utrecht, the Netherlands and is one of the best European players. At SMASH ATTACK, he beat Amsah in winners' semis and also managed to beat Armada in winners' finals, though he still ended up losing two sets of grand finals against Armada. Zgetto is currently ranked 13th on the European Melee Power Rankings and 89th on the 2017 SSBMRank, and was formerly ranked 2nd on the Dutch Melee Power Rankings.
