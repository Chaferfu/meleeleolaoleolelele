      Real name     Lucas Blomgren

   Zoler is a Swedish Melee Fox main. He is currently ranked 9th on the Swedish Melee Power Rankings, 4th on the Gothenburg Power Rankings and 22nd on the European Melee Power Rankings. He defeated the best player from the UK Professor Pro in a crew battle at Heir II the Throne. He has also defeated players such as Fuzzyness. However he has been upset many times by players such as ZetTroxX, Legrats, IrregularJinny, MikeHagger, and Tukaani.
