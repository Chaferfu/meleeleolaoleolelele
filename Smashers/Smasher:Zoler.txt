   Zoler is a Swedish [14]Melee [15]Fox main. He is currently ranked 9th on the [16]Swedish Melee Power Rankings, 4th on the [17]Gothenburg Power Rankings and 22nd on the [18]European Melee Power Rankings. He defeated the best player from the UK [19]Professor Pro in a crew battle at [20]Heir II the Throne. He has also defeated players such as [21]Fuzzyness. However he has been upset many times by players such as [22]ZetTroxX, [23]Legrats, [24]IrregularJinny, [25]MikeHagger, and [26]Tukaani.
