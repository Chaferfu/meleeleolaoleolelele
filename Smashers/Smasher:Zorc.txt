   Zorc is a Norwegian [23]Melee [24]Falco main from Oslo, Norway. He is currently ranked 3rd on the [25]Norwegian Power Rankings, 24th on the [26]European Melee Power Rankings, and was formerly ranked 16th on the [27]NorCal Melee Power Rankings. He is considered to be one of the best Falco mains in Europe, as he has taken sets off notable smashers such as [28]Laudandus, [29]HomeMadeWaffles, [30]NMW and [31]Nintendude.
