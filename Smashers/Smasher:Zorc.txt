   Zorc is a Norwegian Melee Falco main from Oslo, Norway. He is currently ranked 3rd on the Norwegian Power Rankings, 24th on the European Melee Power Rankings, and was formerly ranked 16th on the NorCal Melee Power Rankings. He is considered to be one of the best Falco mains in Europe, as he has taken sets off notable smashers such as Laudandus, HomeMadeWaffles, NMW and Nintendude.
