   Apex 2014 was the fifth edition of the [35]Apex tournament series, a fighting game [36]tournament held annually in New Jersey, which took place on January 17th-19th, 2014, at the [37]DoubleTree Hotel.

   Despite its focus on the [38]Smash Bros series, the event also hosted other popular fighting games and a competitive [39]Pokémon X and Y tournament. Apex 2014 currently stands as [40]one of the largest Smash tournaments to date with 629 entrants for [41]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [42]the second largest tournament to date for [43]Project M (behind [44]Paragon Los Angeles 2015), with 382 entrants.
