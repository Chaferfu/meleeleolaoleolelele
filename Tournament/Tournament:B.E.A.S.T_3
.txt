   B.E.A.S.T 3 was a European [24]Melee and [25]Project M tournament held by [26]Lolex in Gothenburg, Sweden.

   The tournament was originally scheduled to be held during January 2nd-6th, 2013, but was withheld until later due to the dates being only a week before [27]Apex 2013. [28]Armada who was retired at the time attended, but did not enter Melee singles or teams only entering PM singles and commentating the grand finals set between [29]Mango and [30]Ice.
