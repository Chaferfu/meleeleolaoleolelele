   B.E.A.S.T 5 was a [27]national tournament held by [28]Lolex and [29]Armada in Gothenburg, Sweden, featuring singles and doubles for [30]Super Smash Bros. Melee, singles for [31]Project M, and singles for [32]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U. It is the European [33]Apex 2015 qualifier, and was also the largest European Melee tournament of all time with 375 entrants for Melee singles, surpassing the previous record of 231 set by [34]Epita Smash Arena 2 in February 2008, until it was surpassed by [35]B.E.A.S.T 6 the following year.

   It features top players from all over Europe, such as [36]Armada, [37]Leffen, [38]Ice, [39]Android, [40]Amsah, [41]Jeapie, [42]Zgetto, [43]Calle W, [44]Salepate, [45]Vdogg, [46]Thomas, [47]Tomber, [48]Professor Pro, and [49]Cyve, as well as top American player [50]Mango and American commentator [51]D1. It is streamed by [52]Liva on [53]VGBootCamp's channel.
