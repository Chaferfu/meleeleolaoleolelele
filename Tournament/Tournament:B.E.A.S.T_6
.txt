   B.E.A.S.T 6 was a [33]national tournament held by [34]Lolex, [35]Isa, and [36]VJ in Gothenburg, Sweden, featuring singles and doubles for [37]Super Smash Bros. Melee, singles for [38]Project M, and singles and doubles for [39]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U. It is the sixth installment of the [40]B.E.A.S.T series, and is currently the [41]largest Swedish Smash tournament of all time.
