   B.E.A.S.T II
   Info
   Dates August 11th-14th, 2011
   Venue Nya Påvelundsskolan
   Address/City Ängkärrsvägen 22, 426 77 Västra Frölunda
   Gothenburg, Sweden [8]Sweden
   Attendance 112+
   Entry fees Melee singles: 100 SEK
   Melee doubles: 100 SEK per person
   Venue: 100 SEK
   Pot size(s) Singles: 11,400 SEK ($1,300.97 USD)
   Doubles:10,400 SEK ($1,186.85 USD)
   Results
   [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee winners [10]Sweden  [11]Armada 1v1
   [12]Sweden  [13]Aniolas & [14]Sweden  [15]Armada 2v2
   Staff
   [16]Director(s) [17]Lolex, [18]Armada

   B.E.A.S.T II was a European [19]national tournament held by [20]Lolex in Gothenburg, Sweden. It was a part of the [21]Road to Apex 2012.
