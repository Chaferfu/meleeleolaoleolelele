Trivia[[224]edit]

     * Bad Moon Rising 2 was notable for being the first event Melee [225]God [226]PPMD has attended since [227]Battle of the Five Gods, although he only participated in doubles.
          + [228]Plup announced on Twitter^[229][1] he would only use [230]Falco in the singles tournament as a tribute to PPMD.
     * In the singles event, top 16 placers were either refunded the entry fee, or both the venue and entry fee (13th and 9th finishes respectively).
     * There was also a bonus contest in which the person determined to have driven (not flown) the longest distance (according to Google Maps) received $100, with the runner-up receiving $50.
     * [231]Qerb received the highest placing for a [232]Mr. Game and Watch main at a major tournament, placing 13th out of 369 entrants.
