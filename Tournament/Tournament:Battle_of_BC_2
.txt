   Battle of BC 2 was the second edition of the [28]Battle of BC tournament series. It was held in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, by the [29]UBC Esports Association on June 3rd-4th, 2017. The event featured singles and doubles tournaments for [30]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [31]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U.
