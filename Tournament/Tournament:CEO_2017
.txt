   CEO 2017, officially Community Effort Orlando 2017, was the 8th edition of the [22]CEO tournament series, a fighting game tournament held annually in Orlando, Florida, and organized by [23]Alex Jebailey. It took place on June 16th-18th, 2017, at the Wyndham Orlando Resort.^[24][1] In late January, Jebailey confirmed that both [25]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U and [26]Super Smash Bros. Melee would be returning to the lineup.^[27][2]^[28][3]
