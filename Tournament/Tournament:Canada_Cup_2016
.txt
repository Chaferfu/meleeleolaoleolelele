   Canada Cup 2016 was the seventh tournament of the [28]Canada Cup series, an annual fighting game tournament held in Toronto, Ontario, organized by Canada Cup Gaming. It was a [29]Super Smash Bros. Melee [30]national and a [31]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U [32]regional and featured singles and doubles events for both games. Lap Chi Duong, one of the organizers, provided donations for [33]Hungrybox and [34]Armada to attend.
