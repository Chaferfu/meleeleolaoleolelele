   Canada Cup 2017 was the eighth installment of the [28]Canada Cup series, an annual fighting game tournament held in Ontario, organized by Canada Cup Gaming. It featured singles and doubles events for both [29]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [30]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U.
