   Cataclysm 3
   Info
   Dates March 3rd-4th, 2007
   Venue David Prouty High School
   Address/City 302 Main Street
   Spencer, Massachusetts [8]USA
   Attendance 112+
   Entry fees Venue: $5 per day
   Singles: $15
   Doubles: $10 per person
   Draft crews: $10 per person
   Pot size(s) Singles: $1,680
   Doubles: $1,160
   Results
   [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee winners [10]USA  [11]Mew2King 1v1
   [12]USA  [13]Mew2King & [14]USA  [15]Isai 2v2
   Staff
   [16]Director(s) The Mages

   Cataclysm 3 was a [17]Super Smash Bros. Melee [18]national tournament held by The Mages during March 3rd-4th, 2007, in Spencer, Massachusetts. It is notable for being the first major tournament won by [19]Mew2King.
