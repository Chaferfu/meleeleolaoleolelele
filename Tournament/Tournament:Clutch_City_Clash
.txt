   Clutch City Clash was a [34]Super Smash Bros. Melee, [35]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U and [36]Project M [37]national tournament held by Smash United in The Woodlands, Texas, during August 6th to 7th, 2016. It was the first 3 stock national in the United States. It was livestreamed by [38]Tourney Locator and Smash United. Numerous upsets happened at this event, with the most notable ones being [39]SFAT double eliminating [40]Mango in Melee and [41]Samsora double eliminating [42]Ally in Smash 4.
