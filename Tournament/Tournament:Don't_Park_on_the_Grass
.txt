   Don't Park on the Grass was a [35]national tournament held in Seattle, Washington, during December 17th-18th, 2016. It was the largest event thus far held in the Pacific Northwest region, and hosted both singles and doubles tournaments for [36]Super Smash Bros., [37]Super Smash Bros. Melee, and [38]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U. It was also [39]one of the largest Melee tournaments of all time, boasting 640 entrants, and stands as the second largest two-day Melee tournament ever, behind [40]Pound 2016.
