   DreamHack Denver 2017 was a [31]national tournament that took place from October 20th-22nd, 2017 at the National Western Complex in Denver, Colorado. It is the fifth event in the DreamHack Smash Championship 2017 season, a series spanning events in North America and Sweden and featuring $100,000 in prize money in total.
