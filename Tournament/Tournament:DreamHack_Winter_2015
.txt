   DreamHack Winter 2015, or simply DHW15, was a [17]national tournament which took place on November 26th-29th, 2015, at Elmia, Jönköping, Sweden. On August 21st, DreamHack announced the inclusion of [18]Super Smash Bros. Melee to its game lineup at DHW15, hosting both singles and doubles tournaments, with a $30,000 prize.

   Due to a schedule conflict with [19]HTC Throwdown, which took place on the same weekend as [20]DreamHack London 2015, the top 2 players in each tournament were invited to DHW15, with all travel costs covered.^[21][1]
