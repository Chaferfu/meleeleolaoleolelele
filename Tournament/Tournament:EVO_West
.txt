   EVO West
   Info
   Dates July 27th-29th, 2007
   Venue Omni San Diego Hotel
   Address/City 675 L Street
   San Diego, California
   Attendance 128 (entry cap)
   Entry fees free with $30 Comic Con pass
   Pot size(s) $1,280
   Results
   [6]Super Smash Bros. Melee winners [7]USA  [8]Mew2King
   Staff
   [9]Director(s) EVO

   EVO West was a tournament in San Diego, California that was part of the 2007 EVO Circuit. It was held at [10]San Diego Comic Con.
