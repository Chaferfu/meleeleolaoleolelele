   Evolution 2007, officially EVO World 2007, or simply known as EVO 2007 was the 6th edition of the [15]Evolution Championship Series, a fighting game tournament held annually in Las Vegas, Nevada, which took place on August 24th-26th, 2007, at the [16]Green Valley Ranch. It was the final tournament of the [17]EVO 2007 circuit, which consisted of [18]EVO South, [19]EVO North, [20]EVO West, and [21]EVO East. The top 8 finishers for each of the previous events were automatically qualified for the bracket round at EVO World.^[22][1] It was the largest [23]Melee tournament before the international release of [24]Brawl with 270 players in attendance.

Tournament Summary[[29]edit]

   Some notable upsets occurred during this tournament. [30]Mango who at the time was a [31]Jigglypuff [32]main on the come up beat [33]Mew2King in pools, then considered to be the best player in the world. Mew2King then lost to [34]Chillin in the losers top 8 qualifier to finish at an underwhelming 9th place. Mango continued his run by upsetting [35]Ken in winners-semis. He then lost to [36]HugS in winners finals and Ken in a losers finals rematch to finish at 3rd place. Ken, after getting upset by Mango, made a losers run beating [37]ChuDat, [38]PC Chris, Mango, and HugS in 2 sets of grand finals to win the tournament. After the tournament many analysts wrote off Mango's win against Mew2King and 3rd place finish as a fluke until his win at [39]Pound 3.
