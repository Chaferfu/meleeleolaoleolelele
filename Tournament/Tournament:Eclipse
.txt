   Eclipse was a Norwegian [21]Super Smash Bros. Melee [22]national and [23]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U regional tournament held on November 14th-15th, 2015. As it took place only a couple of weeks before [24]DreamHack Winter 2015, it featured competition from outside the continent such as [25]Axe, [26]Plup and [27]Westballz. [28]Wobbles also participated in the event as a commentator. This tournament was notably [29]Leffen's first national tournament attendance since [30]HTC Throwdown, after visa complications stopped him from participating in major American tournaments [31]The Big House 5 and [32]Smash Summit.
