   Eclipse 2 was a [27]Super Smash Bros. Melee [28]national and [29]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U super regional tournament held in Jessheim, Norway, during October 21st-23rd, 2016. It currently stands as [30]the largest Norwegian Smash tournament to date, with over 13 countries being represented amongst the tournament's attendees, which included [31]Armada, [32]Leffen, [33]Professor Pro, [34]Fuzzyness, [35]Ice, [36]Kage, [37]Chillin, [38]Mr. R and [39]iStudying, and featured the first national tournament appearance of [40]Captain Jack since [41]Apex 2012.
