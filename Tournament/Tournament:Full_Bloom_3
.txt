   Full Bloom 3 was a [27]national [28]Super Smash Bros. Melee and regional [29]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U tournament that was held in Bloomington, Indiana on March 25th, 2017. Several out-of-region top players were in attendance, such as [30]Hungrybox, [31]Leffen, [32]Westballz, [33]SFAT, [34]The Moon, and [35]Ice.
