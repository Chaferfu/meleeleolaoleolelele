   Get On My Level 2014, referred to as Get On My Level before the announcement of [27]its sequel, was a [28]Super Smash Bros. Melee [29]national tournament held by [30]Toronto Joe in Toronto, Ontario during May 10th-11th, 2014. It also featured a [31]Super Smash Bros. Brawl event using a one stock, three minute ruleset (rather than the usual three stock, eight minute ruleset). It was sponsored by [32]Major League Gaming and was a qualifier for the Melee event at [33]MLG Anaheim 2014.
