   Get On My Level 2016 was the 3rd edition of the [40]Get On My Level series, a Super Smash Bros tournament held annually in Canada. It took place May 20th to 22nd, 2016, at the Queen Elizabeth Building, Toronto. The event hosted tournaments for all four main Smash games, with 987 unique entrants registred, making it [41]the largest Canadian Smash tournament to date. It was also part of a larger event called Good Game Con, which included several tournaments for other eSports, as well as Toryuken 5 running other fighting games.

   After a disappointing 7th place finish in his previous visit to Toronto at [42]Enthusiast Gaming Live Expo, [43]Leffen scored a resounding victory in Melee singles, taking successive wins over [44]Mew2King, [45]Armada, [46]Hungrybox, and [47]Mango. [48]n0ne also won against Mew2King in a huge upset, becoming the first [49]Captain Falcon player to defeat him in over a decade. However, he narrowly lost to [50]Lucky in a last game, last stock situation, earning a 7th place finish for himself.

   [51]ZeRo made his return to Smash 4 tournament play early after [52]Zinoto dropped out of GOML 2016 due to personal reasons, and defeated [53]San, [54]Mr. R, [55]Leo, [56]Nairo, and [57]Larry Lurr after being sent to losers bracket early in winners quarters by Mew2King, who became the first smasher in Smash 4 history to defeat ZeRo with a 3-0 victory. [58]Ally took his first ever major win for Smash 4 singles, not only taking his first games off of ZeRo in grand finals, but defeating him for the first time 3-2, with each game being won consecutively. He also won Smash 4 doubles with [59]ANTi and Brawl singles over [60]V115 and ANTi. Leo entered GOML 2016 shortly after registration ended due to widespread community approval, and played against ZeRo in losers quarters in a highly anticipated set. He ended up losing to ZeRo 2-3, netting a 5th place finish.

   At his very first offline tournament, Smash 64 player [61]Derek, after losing in pools, defeated well known players such as [62]Shears, [63]Fireblaster, [64]Nintendude, and [65]BarkSanchez to place 5th after losing to [66]The Z 2-3.
