   Helix is a [22]national [23]Super Smash Bros. Melee and a regional [24]Project M tournament that took place on September 26th-27th, 2015, at the Microsoft Atrium in Berlin, Germany and directed by the Game 5 team. It is the first national tournament to be hosted in Germany since [25]SMASH ATTACK six years prior, and is the seventh-largest European Melee tournament of all time, behind [26]Heir 3, [27]B.E.A.S.T 6, [28]B.E.A.S.T 5, [29]Heir II the Throne, [30]Epita Smash Arena 2 and [31]Syndicate 2016, in order.
