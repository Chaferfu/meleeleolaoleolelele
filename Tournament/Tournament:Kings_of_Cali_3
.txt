   Kings of Cali 3 was a West Coast [24]Super Smash Bros. Melee [25]national tournament, held by [26]Team OXY in Pasadena, California. It was the third tournament of the Kings of Cali tournament series.

   One of the more notable events that occurred at this tournament was [27]Fly Amanita's run through the losers' bracket. After being knocked out of the winners' bracket 1-3 by [28]Mew2King in the second round, he eliminated [29]Darrell 3-0, [30]Fiction 3-1, [31]Sung 3-0, [32]Silent Wolf 3-2, [33]Westballz 3-1, [34]Axe 3-2, and [35]Shroomed 3-2 before reaching grand finals against Mew2King.
