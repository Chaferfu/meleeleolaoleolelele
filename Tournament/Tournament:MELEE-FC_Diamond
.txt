   MELEE-FC Diamond, or FCD, was a [23]Super Smash Bros. Melee [24]national tournament held by the [25]Ship of Fools group during July 12th-14th, 2007, in South Bend, Indiana. It was sponsored by [26]Major League Gaming, and was the third tournament in the MLG Smash Series circuit of 2007.

   With a total of 256 entrants for singles, it was the [27]largest Melee tournament of all time until it was surpassed by [28]EVO World 2007.

   With a total of 64 entrants for Smash 64 singles, it was the also the [29]largest Smash 64 tournament for a few years.
