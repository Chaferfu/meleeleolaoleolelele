   MLG New York Opener 2006 also known as MLG Meadowlands 2006 was a [18]Super Smash Bros. Melee [19]national tournament held by [20]Major League Gaming in Secaucus, New Jersey, during April 21st-23rd, 2006. It was the first tournament in the MLG 2006 circuit.
