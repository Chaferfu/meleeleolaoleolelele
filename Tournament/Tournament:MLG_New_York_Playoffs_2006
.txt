   MLG New York Playoffs 2006 also known as MLG Manhattan 2006 was a [18]Super Smash Bros. Melee [19]national tournament held by [20]Major League Gaming in New York City, New York, during October 13th-14th, 2006. It was the sixth tournament in the MLG 2006 circuit.
