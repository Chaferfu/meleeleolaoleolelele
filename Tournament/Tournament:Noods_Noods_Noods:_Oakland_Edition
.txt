   Noods Noods Noods: Oakland Edition was a national [19]Super Smash Bros. Melee and super regional [20]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U tournament that took place on April 7th, 2018 in Oakland, California. The tournament was part of a larger event, an Asian food festival, also named Noods Noods Noods. As such, it was sponsored by [21]Foodbeast and [22]Cup Noodles. The tournament was a follow-up to [23]Noods Noods Noods: Melee Edition.
