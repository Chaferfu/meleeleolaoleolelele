   PAX Arena was a [30]national tournament held in San Antonio from January 29th to 31st, 2016, at the [31]PAX South 2016 video game convention. It was hosted by veteran tournament organizers [32]TheCrimsonBlur (TO of Kings of Cali) and [33]Juggleguy (TO of The Big House), featuring singles and doubles for [34]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [35]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U. The tournament was free to enter for all PAX South attendees, and featured a $10,000 prize pot provided by [36]Twitch, with a live stream on Twitch by [37]VGBootCamp.^[38][1]
