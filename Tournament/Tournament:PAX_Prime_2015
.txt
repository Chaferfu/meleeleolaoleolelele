   Smash @ PAX Prime was an [28]national tournament held in Seattle, Washington, from August 28th to 30th, 2015, at the [29]PAX Prime 2015 video game convention. It was hosted by veteran tournament organizers [30]TheCrimsonBlur and [31]Nintendude, featuring singles and doubles for [32]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [33]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U. The tournament was free to enter for all PAX Prime attendees, and featured a $10,000 prize pot provided by [34]Twitch, with a live stream on Twitch by [35]VGBootCamp.^[36][1]
