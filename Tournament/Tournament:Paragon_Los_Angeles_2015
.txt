   Paragon Los Angeles 2015, or simply Paragon LA 2015, was the second edition of the [26]Paragon tournament series, a [27]national tournament which took place on September 5th to 6th, 2015, at the Ontario Convention Center in Los Angeles, California.

   The event was held by [28]Versus and [29]Gaming Knights, featuring singles for [30]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [31]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U and singles and doubles for [32]Project M, with live streams by [33]VGBootCamp, [34]Versus and Project M Central. Paragon LA currently stands as [35]one of the largest Smash tournaments to date and the second largest Project M tournament to date, with 405 entrants. Due to unforeseen circumstances, the doubles tournaments for Melee and Smash Wii U were canceled. ^[36][1] Despite being allowed to progress, Project M doubles was unable to be completed before the end of the tournament, ending in a forced split between the six remaining teams.
