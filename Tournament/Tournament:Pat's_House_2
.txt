   Pat's House 2 was a [19]Super Smash Bros. Melee [20]national tournament tournament held by [21]Azn Lep in La Jolla, San Diego, California during May 24th-25th, 2014. It was sponsored by [22]Major League Gaming and was a 2000 point qualifier for the Melee event at [23]MLG Anaheim 2014.
