   Pound 2016, also known as Pound VI, was the sixth main event in the [37]Pound tournament series created by [38]Plank, and the first major Pound tournament since [39]Pound V in 2011. The event took place in McLean, Virginia from April 2-3, 2016. Tournaments were hosted by [40]Plank in conjunction with [41]Nintendude and [42]TheTantalus, from the [43]VGBootCamp team.^[44][1]

Summary[[59]edit]

   Plank, Pound 2016's tournament organizer, was the only tournament organizer featured on the Tournament Organizer Blacklist due to his inability to pay out players at Pound V in 2011.^[60][2]^[61][3] Plank has since been removed from the blacklist, after several players reported receiving payment from Pound V.^[62][4]^[63][5]^[64][6] Registration was officially opened on December 4th, 2015.

   Pound 2016 currently stands as [65]one of the largest Smash Bros tournaments to date, with 933 entrants for [66]Melee and 514 entrants (the maximum number) for [67]Smash 4.

   With [68]ZeRo's absence from the Smash 4 event due to medical issues, [69]Abadango won his first major American tournament using [70]Mewtwo, a character generally considered to be mid [71]tier at the time. [72]Dabuz failed to make top eight while [73]Nairo was eliminated early on at a disappointing 49th place, his worst placing at a major so far. [74]Marss and [75]Pink Fresh also notably placed 3rd and 7th, respectively. In the Melee event, [76]Hungrybox took his third major in a row without dropping a set, losing only a single game to [77]Mango in grand finals; Mango additionally fell to [78]Hax in winners' semis, though he later ousted him in losers' finals. [79]Swedish Delight and [80]Laudandus both made unexpected top eight finishes while [81]InfiniteNumbers placed 9th at his first-ever major, beating [82]DJ Nintendo, [83]Alex19, [84]MacD, and [85]Mike Haze; [86]Axe and [87]Westballz also suffered early losses, placing 25th and 33rd respectively.

Trivia[[925]edit]

     * The 512 player cap for Smash 4 was exceeded by 1 due to a player from the rewards shop being added after the cap had been hit.
     * Pound 2016 stands as [926]Nairo's worst placing in Smash 4, at 49th place.
     * This was the first Smash 4 American major won by a Japanese player, Abadango.
          + This also means it was the first Smash 4 American major not won by [927]ZeRo or Nairo.
          + This was also the first Smash 4 national tournament to be won with [928]Mewtwo in grand finals.
