   Revival of Melee was a [19]Super Smash Bros. Melee [20]national tournament held by [21]Alukard in Nanuet, New York, during March 7th-8th, 2009. It was the inaugural tournament of the Revival of Melee series.

   This tournament was the first major gathering of the best Melee players in North America since [22]Super Smash Bros. Brawl's release a year earlier. The tournament is also known for the fourth game of the losers' finals set between [23]Mew2King and [24]DaShizWiz, which is regarded as one of the greatest recorded Melee matches of all time because of M2K's impeccable 3-1 comeback coupled with the reaction of the announcer. [25]The video of the match has attracted over 900,000 views on [26]YouTube since it was uploaded.
