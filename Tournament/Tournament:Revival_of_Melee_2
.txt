   Revival of Melee 2 was a [18]Super Smash Bros. Melee [19]national tournament held by [20]Alukard in Nanuet, New York, during November 21st-22nd, 2009.

   This tournament is known for [21]Kage's upsets, in which he double eliminated [22]Mango (the player considered the best in the world at the time), defeating him 2-0 in winners' quarters and 2-1 in losers' semis. [23]Dr. PeePee also became known for his [24]Falco placing higher than Mango's at this tournament.
