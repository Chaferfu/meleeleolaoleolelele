   Revival of Melee 4
   Info
   Dates November 19th-20th, 2011
   Venue Toy Wiz
   Address/City 253 West Route 59
   Nanuet, New York [8]USA
   Attendance 110+
   Entry fees Singles: $20
   Doubles: $10 per person
   Venue: $20 (for competitors) $10 or $15 (if setup was brought)
   Pot size(s) Singles: $2,200
   Results
   [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee winners [10]USA  [11]Mango 1v1
   [12]Canada  [13]Kage & [14]USA  [15]Mew2King 2v2
   Staff
   [16]Director(s) [17]Alukard

   Revival of Melee 4, or ROM 4, was an Atlantic North [18]Super Smash Bros. Melee national tournament and [19]Apex 2012 qualifier held by [20]Alukard in Nanuet, New York.
