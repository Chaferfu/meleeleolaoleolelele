   Revival of Melee 6, also known as ROM 6, was the sixth tournament in the Revival of Melee tournament series, hosted from November 16th to 17th, 2013 in Nanuet, New York by [19]Alukard. It was an [20]Apex 2014 qualifier.

   One particular upset in the tournament was [21]The Moon making by far his most notable placement so far, defeating top players such as [22]Chillindude829 to come in 3rd place.
