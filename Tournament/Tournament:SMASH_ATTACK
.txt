          This article is about the tournament. For the type of attack, see [23]smash attack.

   SMASH ATTACK (short: SMACK) was a European [24]national tournament held by [25]°UÇK during April 3rd-5th, 2009 in Bremen, Germany.

   A notable event that occurred at this tournament was [26]Zgetto beating [27]Armada 4-2 in winners' finals. However, Zgetto ended up losing both sets of grand finals to Armada 0-4.
