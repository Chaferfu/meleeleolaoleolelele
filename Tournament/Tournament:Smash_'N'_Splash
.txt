   Smash 'N' Splash was a [36]Super Smash Bros. Melee [37]national and a [38]Project M and [39]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U regional tournament held by [40]Sveet for Melee, Alex Nesler for Project M and [41]Yink for Smash Wii U in Gurnee, Illinois during June 13th-14th, 2015. True to its namesake, the tournament was hosted inside a waterpark resort.
