   Smash 'N' Splash 2 was a [44]national tournament in Gurnee, Illinois that took place during June 11th-12th, 2016. Like [45]Smash 'N' Splash, the tournament was held at [46]Key Lime Cove, an indoor waterpark resort. It was the fifth event in the 2016 [47]Project M Championship Circuit.
