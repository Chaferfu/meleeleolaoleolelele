   Smash Needs You
   [8]Smash Needs You logo.jpg
   Info
   Dates August 10th-12th, 2010
   Venue Bilberry Hill Centre
   Address/City Rose Hill
   Lickey, Birmingham [9]UK
   Entry fees Venue: £12
   Melee singles: £5
   Melee doubles: £5 per person
   Results
   [10]Super Smash Bros. Melee winners [11]Sweden  [12]Armada 1v1
   [13]Sweden  [14]Armada & [15]Sweden  [16]Aniolas 2v2
   Staff
   [17]Director(s) [18]Kone
   Assisted by AMCD, [19]Fuzzyness, Jo3, Tamoo, Aiko, Bullet Bill

   Smash Needs You was a European [20]national tournament held by [21]Kone during August 10th-12th, 2010 in Lickey, Birmingham.
