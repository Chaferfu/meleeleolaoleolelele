   Smash Rivalries was an invitational [22]Super Smash Bros. Melee tournament held on April 8th-9th, 2017 and featured a $15,000 prize pot ($5000 per event) provided by Yahoo Esports. The event was inspired by the East Coast/West Coast rivalries and specifically the [23]crew battles which took place at landmark tournaments such as [24]MELEE-FC3, [25]GENESIS, [26]GENESIS 2, and [27]MLG Anaheim 2014.
