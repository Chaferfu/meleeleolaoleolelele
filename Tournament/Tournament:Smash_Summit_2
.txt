   Smash Summit 2 was a [18]national [19]Super Smash Bros. Melee invitational [20]tournament held in Los Angeles, California from April 21st-24th, 2016 by Beyond the Summit, an eSports company known for their [21]Dota 2 coverage. Singles, doubles and side events for Melee were hosted. The tournament was held at the Summit gaming house, a private residence, and so is not open to the general public. This tournament is also notable for being only the second tournament where Mew2King beat Armada, the other being [22]SKTAR 3.

Crews and Smash Olympics[[243]edit]

   At the very beginning of Smash Summit 2, 4 captains who were previously chosen by vote (Hungrybox, Mango, Armada, and Westballz) formed crews by picking smashers through a snake draft process.
   Team Pandabox  [244]USA  [245]Hungrybox [246]USA  [247]Plup     [248]USA  [249]Wobbles     [250]USA  [251]ESAM
   Team Mango     [252]USA  [253]Mango     [254]USA  [255]S2J      [256]USA  [257]Silent Wolf [258]USA  [259]Duck
   Team Armada    [260]Sweden  [261]Armada [262]Germany  [263]Ice  [264]USA  [265]SFAT        [266]USA  [267]Shroomed
   Team Westballz [268]USA  [269]Westballz [270]USA  [271]Mew2King [272]USA  [273]Axe         [274]USA  [275]PewPewU

   Each crew would gain points when their members won or placed high enough at the verious side events such as the Shared Controller Tournament and the Iron Man Challenge. The team with the most points at the end would win the Smash Olympics and earn $750 to share among themselves.

Trivia[[583]edit]

     * This tournament was the first time ever that Mew2King defeated Armada in a best of 5 set, winning 3-0 in Winners Semis (although Armada ended up defeating him 3-0 in Losers Finals).
          + The last time Mew2King took a set off Armada was in 2014 at [584]SKTAR 3.
     * This was the very first Melee tournament in California that both [585]Armada and [586]Mango entered where they did not meet in Grand Finals.
