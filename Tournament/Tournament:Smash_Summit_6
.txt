   Smash Summit 6 was a [18]national [19]Super Smash Bros. Melee invitational tournament and the sixth installment of the [20]Smash Summit tournament series, held on May 3rd-6th, 2018. Like previous Smash Summits, it was held at the Summit gaming house, a private residence house in Los Angeles, California, and was closed to the general public.

Eligible players[[115]edit]

   Anyone ranked in the [116]2017 SSBMRank Top 100 and those who placed Top 64 (49th or better) at [117]GENESIS 5 or Top 32 (25th or better) at either [118]EGLX 2018 or [119]Full Bloom 4 were eligible to be nominated. 111 players were eligible, which was 35 less than [120]Smash Summit 5â€™s number of eligible players. The 20 players with the most Twitter nominations progressed to the voting phase.

Trivia[[395]edit]

     * Zain's third-place finish marks the highest ever placing by a voted-in player at any Smash Summit event to date.
          + The record was previously held by [396]Duck, who placed seventh at [397]Smash Summit 3, and [398]ChuDat, who placed seventh at [399]Smash Summit Spring 2017.
               o In addition, aMSa placed seventh at this event, which would have added a third tie to the previous record.
     * This is the first Smash Summit that [400]S2J did not participate in after being voted in every single Smash Summit previously.
