   Smash Summit 7 was a [18]national [19]Super Smash Bros. Melee invitational tournament. It was the seventh installment of the [20]Smash Summit series, and was held on November 15th-18th, 2018. Like previous Smash Summits, it was held at the Summit Gaming House, a private residence house in Los Angeles, California, and was closed to the general public.

Eligible players[[115]edit]

   Anyone ranked on the [116]2017 SSBMRank Top 100 or [117]Summer 2018 MPGR Top 50, or placing in Top 64 (33rd or better) at [118]EVO 2018; Top 32 (25th or better) at [119]Get On My Level 2018, [120]Smash 'N' Splash 4, [121]CEO 2018, [122]Super Smash Con 2018, [123]Shine 2018, and [124]The Big House 8; or Top 16 (13th or better) at [125]Low Tier City 6 and [126]Heir 5 are eligible for nomination. [127]Nightmare is presumably unqualified for nomination because of his ban from competition.

Trivia[[390]edit]

     * This is the first time [391]Armada was not participating at a Smash Summit tournament, having announced his retirement from Melee singles a few months prior.
          + This is also the first time [392]Axe was not initially invited to Smash Summit, although he later qualified by being the second highest non-qualifier at The Big House 8.
     * Smash Summit 7 is the first Summit tournament where a non-US smasher was not voted in, breaking the trend that started in the original Smash Summit.
          + This is also the first Summit tournament to only have five people voted in, as opposed to the usual six, since Beyond the Summit invited eleven players.
