   Smash Summit Spring 2017 was a [18]national [19]Super Smash Bros. Melee invitational tournament and the fourth installment of the [20]Smash Summit tournament series, held on March 2nd-5th, 2017. Like previous Smash Summits, it was held at the Summit gaming house, a private residence house in Los Angeles, California, and was closed to the general public.

Format[[257]edit]

   Unlike previous installments, the singles bracket was not decided by initial seeding but through four group stages in the GSL format, which is a four player double-elimination bracket without a grand final. The pool order was decided by the top four seeds (Armada, Hungrybox, Mango and Mew2King) who selected players in snake order.

Trivia[[557]edit]

     * A Summit tournament organizer confirmed that the Summit team decided against numbering Smash Summit events after the [558]third entry.^[559][1] However, the fifth event was revealed to be named [560]Smash Summit 5, returning to being numbered.
     * [561]Leffen's name was uncommonly placed as "L3ffen" on the first promotional image for the tournament, which happens to be how his YouTube and Instagram username is stylized.
