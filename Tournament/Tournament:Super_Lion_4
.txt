   Super Lion 4 was a European national Melee and Brawl tournament in Salle Didier Latimier, Parc du Vellein, Villefontaine, France 38090 from August 7-9, 2009. Official events were Melee singles and doubles, Brawl singles and doubles, and a Melee All Star Crew battle. It was organized by porc, Salepâte and Slhoka, and helped by Achoral.

   Despite the lack of interest for Brawl, it was the first European sized Smash Bros. tournament to feature both Brawl and Melee events. The hosts made this effort to try to reunite both communities. While the tournament was a success in both games, the Brawl events lasted too long, and a lot of time that was originally planned for Melee events was used for Brawl events.

   Because of this, as most of the TOs were more focused on Melee, they decided to not make any Brawl events in the other Super Lion tournaments that followed this one. Their efforts for the Brawl events were still recognized by the French Brawl community.
