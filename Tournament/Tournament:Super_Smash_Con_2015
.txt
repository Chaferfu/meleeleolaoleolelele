   Super Smash Con 2015, referred to simply as Super Smash Con before the announcement of [45]its sequel, was the inaugural Super Smash Con, a video game convention and tournament focused on the [46]Super Smash Bros. series, held in Chantilly, Virginia, from August 6th-9th, 2015, at the Dulles Expo Center.

   The tournament portion of the event currently stands as [47]one of the largest Smash tournaments to date. Super Smash Con was hosted by [48]Justin Wykowski; tournaments were planned by veteran tournament organizers [49]Nintendude and [50]Juggleguy, and live streamed on [51]Twitch by [52]VGBootCamp.
