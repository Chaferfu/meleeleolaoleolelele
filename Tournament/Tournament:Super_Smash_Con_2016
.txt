   Super Smash Con 2016 was the 2nd edition of the "Super Smash Con", a video game convention and national tournament focused on the [45]Super Smash Bros. series, held in Chantilly, Virginia, from August 11th-14th, 2016. The event hosted singles and doubles tournaments for all four Smash games, and is currently [46]the largest Smash 64 tournament to date and the fifth largest Smash Wii U tournament of all time. Because of this, it is the biggest Smash For Wii U tournament that only had Smash games and/or was not part of the [47]EVO Series.^[48][1]

     * first by sets won
     * second by games won

Trivia[[1302]edit]

     * [1303]TSM-sponsored smashers [1304]Leffen and [1305]ZeRo, both of whom had respectively won [1306]Super Smash Con 2015 in Melee and Smash 4, notably failed to defend their titles.
          + [1307]Leffen could not defend his Melee title in any capacity, due to his ongoing visa issues surrounding his sponsor.
          + [1308]ZeRo finished in 13th after losing to [1309]Day in the winner's bracket and [1310]Ally in the loser's bracket. This finish stood as his worst placing in a Smash 4 tournament until his 49th place finish at [1311]2GGC: Civil War.
     * [1312]Mew2King also failed to defend his title in Brawl singles, losing to [1313]V115 in winner's bracket and [1314]Ally in loser's bracket, finishing at 9th place.
