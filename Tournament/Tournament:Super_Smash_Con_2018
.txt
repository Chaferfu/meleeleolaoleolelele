   Super Smash Con 2018 was the 4th edition of "Super Smash Con", a video game convention and national tournament focused on the [46]Super Smash Bros. series. It was held in Chantilly, Virginia, from August 9th-12th, 2018.

Smash 64 combo contest[[1315]edit]

   Each contestant gets 2 minutes to preform a combo. They can use any character, any stage, and any item. Once the two minutes are up, or when the contestant finishes his or her combo, five judges rate the combo, giving it a number between 1-10. Those points are totalled up into a final number. The higher the score, the better chance in moving on.

   In the first round, the top four contestants move on. In the second round, the top two contestants move on. In the final round, both contestants have to do two combos. Although total points are recorded, whoever moves on is chosen solely through said round's points.

     * first by sets won
     * second by games won
     * third by "big victories": wins where a player defeats the opponent with two or more stocks left.

     * [1454]Mango forfeited after losing to [1455]Flipsy in Round 2 pools, placing 65th, marking his worst placing at a Melee tournament in his career to date.
          + There were also other notable DQ's at the tournament. [1456]Plup could not make it on time for his pools because of his flight, [1457]Leffen fell ill and dropped out before round 2 pools, and [1458]Wizzrobe decided to focus on [1459]SSB as Super Smash Con 2018 was its 2nd largest tournament ever and thus decided not to compete in Melee.
          + This is also [1460]SFAT's worst placing at a Melee tournament in his career, placing 33rd, tied with his placement at [1461]Apex 2013.
          + Additionally, this is four Smash 4 players' worst placing at a tournament in their careers:
               o [1462]Ally, who placed 65th, defeated by [1463]Ralphie before his forfeit.
               o [1464]Dabuz, who placed 33rd, tied with his placement last year at [1465]Super Smash Con 2017.
               o [1466]Light, who placed 97th, tied with his placement at [1467]GENESIS 4.
               o [1468]Raito, who placed 65th, tied with his placement at [1469]Sumabato 24.
     * [1470]Nairo placed 5th in Smash 4 singles, ending his two-year streak of winning the event at Super Smash Con. He lost to [1471]MkLeo using [1472]Cloud and [1473]Bayonetta and [1474]Cosmos using [1475]Corrin, marking his first losses at any Super Smash Con to a character other than [1476]Pikachu.
     * In Smash 4, this tournament marks the highest ever placing of a [1477]Peach main at a major national with [1478]Samsora placing 2nd.
     * Both Melee and Smash 4 grand finals pitted a [1479]Peach player against a [1480]Marth player, with both of the Marths being [1481]Echo Fox players.
