   The Big House 2 was a [24]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [25]Project M tournament held by [26]Juggleguy in Ann Arbor, Michigan. It was a qualifying tournament for [27]Apex 2013.

Melee singles[[34]edit]

   Entrants were split into 16 round robin pools of 8 players each. The top four players from each pool advanced to the next round of pools, in which the top four players in each pool advanced again to a final bracket of 32 players.
