   The Big House 6 was a national [27]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [28]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U tournament held in Dearborn, Michigan from October 7th-9th, 2016. It was the sixth edition of [29]Juggleguy's prestigious Midwest [30]tournament series.

   The competition was fierce for both games. In Melee, the four active [31]Gods [32]Armada, [33]Hungrybox, [34]Mango, and [35]Mew2King were present, as well as all the other American top players such as [36]Plup, [37]Axe, [38]SFAT, [39]Wizzrobe, and [40]PewPewU. Europe was also well represented with [41]Ice, [42]Professor Pro, and [43]Android participating. The Big House 6 was notable for having high-level international attendees such as [44]Rudolph from Japan and [45]Spud from New Zealand. It was also [46]Leffen's first tournament in the United States in over a year, although he ended up [47]sandbagging.

   In [48]Smash 4, 27 of the 50 [49]Panda Global Rankings v1 ranked players were in attendance, including the defending champion [50]ZeRo, the [51]EVO 2016 champion [52]Ally, [53]Nairo, [54]Dabuz, [55]ANTi, [56]Mr.R, [57]VoiD, [58]Abadango, [59]komorikiri, [60]Larry Lurr and many others. Unranked threats such as [61]Kameme (who had recently placed 2nd at EVO) and [62]Salem (who had made his breakthrough in the top echelon of players at [63]Collision XIV) were additional contenders for 1st place.

   The Big House 6 is the [64]seventh largest Smash tournament of all time. It currently stands as the [65]fifth largest Melee tournament of all time as well as the [66]eleventh largest Smash 4 tournament.

Trivia[[1019]edit]

     * The Big House 6 is the first US tournament [1020]Leffen was able to enter since [1021]HTC Throwdown one year before due to issues receiving a worker's visa. As a result, he decided [1022]not to take singles seriously and opted to play Marth and Falco, getting 17th place.
     * This tournament is notable for the many upsets that occurred during Super Smash Bros. Melee's bracket, including [1023]Silent Wolf's victory over [1024]Mew2King, [1025]Zain's victory over [1026]Plup, and [1027]R2DLiu's victory over [1028]Axe.
