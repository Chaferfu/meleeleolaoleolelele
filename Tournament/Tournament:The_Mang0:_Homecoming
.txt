   The Mang0: Homecoming was a national [20]Super Smash Bros. Melee tournament held in Norwalk, California on November 10th-11th, 2018. The tournament was a sequel to [21]The Mango and the second tournament in 2GG's [22]Road to Mango Juice 2 series. [23]Universal Controller Fix was enabled for this tournament.

Trivia[[255]edit]

     * [256]Mango (playing [257]Mario and [258]Captain Falcon) lost to [259]EastCoastJeff in Winners Round 3 of pools and to [260]Venti in losers, placing 65th, marking his worst placing at a Melee tournament in his career to date on par with his forfeit in losers at [261]Super Smash Con 2018.
     * [262]Axe entered chosing random characters and lost to [263]Fluff in Round 2 pools and later to [264]Smashdaddy in losers, placing 49th, marking his second worst placing at a Melee tournament in his career after his 65th place at [265]The Big House 6.
