   The Renaissance of Smash 4 was a [18]Super Smash Bros. Melee [19]national tournament held by [20]Helios during July 3rd-6th, 2007, in Varberg, Sweden. With a total of 132 entrants for singles, it was the largest European tournament of all time until it was surpassed by [21]Epita Smash Arena 2 seven months later.
