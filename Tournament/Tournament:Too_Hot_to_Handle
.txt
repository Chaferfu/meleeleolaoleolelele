   Too Hot To Handle
   Info
   Dates October 28th-29th, 2017
   Venue Miccosukee Resort & Gaming
   Address/City 500 SW 177th Ave
   Miami, Florida [9]USA
   Attendance 367
   Pot size(s) $3,000 bonus (Melee + Wii U singles)
   $2,000 bonus (Melee + Wii U doubles)
   Results
   [10]Super Smash Bros. Melee winners [11]USA  [12]Hungrybox 1v1

   [24]Director(s) [25]Shamunt, [26]MattDotZeb, [27]Bear

   Too Hot To Handle was a [28]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U and [29]Super Smash Bros. Melee [30]national tournament held in Miami, Florida by [31]Trail Gaming League and sponsored by [32]Panda Global. It featured singles and doubles events for both games.
