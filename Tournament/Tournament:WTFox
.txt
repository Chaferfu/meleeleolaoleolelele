   What The Fox, or simply WTFox, was a [34]national [35]Melee [36]tournament and a regional [37]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U and [38]Project M tournament held in Memphis, Tennessee on July 10th-11th, 2015, at the Cook Convention Center as part of the Anime Blues Con 5. Dubbed a "preparation for [39]EVO 2015", Despite the low entrance numbers, WTFox was notable for those who did attend, with many top players present, including four of [40]the top 6 of Melee.
