   WTFox 2, short for What The Fox 2, was the second edition of the WTFox tournament series. It was a [39]national [40]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [41]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U tournament and a local [42]Super Smash Bros. and [43]Project M tournament held at the Memphis Cook Convention Center during July 1st-3rd, 2016. It was livestreamed on [44]Twitch by [45]Smash Studios and Memphis Smash.
