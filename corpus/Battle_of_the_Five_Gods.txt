   Battle of the Five Gods was an invitational [15]Super Smash Bros. Melee tournament held in Austin, Texas from March 17th to 19th, 2016, as part of the SXSW Gaming Expo festival. At the time, the tournament had the [16]highest single-player payout in Smash, with $13,000 awarded to [17]Hungrybox for winning the tournament.

   The name of the tournament references the five "[18]Gods" attending, namely [19]Armada, [20]Hungrybox, [21]Mango, [22]Mew2King and [23]PPMD.

Format[[28]edit]

   The tournament featured a mixed format, with two qualificatory round-robin group stages and a final Top 8 double elimination bracket. The 20 players were first divided in five round-robin groups. The first placing player of each group was sent directly to the Top 8 bracket, while the second placing players were sent to another round-robin group. The top 3 players from this second group would be sent to the final three spots on the Top 8 bracket.

   After the group Top 8 bracket was set, matches were played on a double elimination format. All matches are best 3 out of 5.

Trivia[[99]edit]

     * This was the first tournament since [100]Apex 2010 that Hungrybox won with all 5 gods attending.
     * This was notably [101]PPMD's worst placing since [102]Pound 4, where he got 9th place.
