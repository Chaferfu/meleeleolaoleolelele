   Don't Go Down There Jeff!
   Info
   Dates December 18th-19th, 2010
   Venue Fuddruckers
   Address/City 975 Diamond Blvd # E260
   Concord, California
   Attendance 108
   Entry fees Venue: $10
   Singles: $10
   Doubles: $20 ($10 per person)
   4 or 5 Man crews: $25
   Pot size(s) Singles: $1,080
   Doubles: $840
   Results
   [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee winners [10]USA  [11]Jman 1v1
   [12]USA  [13]Mango and [14]USA  [15]Lucky 2v2
   Staff
   [16]Director(s) [17]HyugaRicdeau

   Don't Go Down There Jeff! also known as Good Shit German 2, was a [18]Melee NorCal regional. The tournament was named after the Grand Finals set between [19]Mango and [20]SilentSpectre at Mango Juice.
