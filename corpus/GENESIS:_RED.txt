   GENESIS: RED was a tournament held on July 9th, 2017 in Berkeley, California, as the final event in the [21]GENESIS [22]FUSE doubles circuit and the last major Melee event before [23]EVO 2017.

   [24]Leffen was in attendance with his static doubles partner [25]Ice. However, he (and other top players) decided not to enter the singles tournament for various reasons.^[26][1]
