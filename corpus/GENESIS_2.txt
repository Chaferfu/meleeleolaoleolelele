   GENESIS 2 was a tournament held by [38]Sidefx and the crew [39]DBR in July 2011, featuring [40]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [41]Super Smash Bros. Brawl singles and doubles as main events, as well as smaller [42]Super Smash Bros. and [43]Project M singles side events. It was the successor of the first [44]GENESIS. Held in Antioch, California and sponsored by Red Bull and SABERGAMING, the tournament had one of the most entrants and prize money ever seen at a tourney, with over 300 attendants and $22,000 in prize money.

   A [45]notorious incident of theft occurred at this tournament, committed by renowned smasher and Death By Rape crew member [46]Zelgadis. After most of the crew members had left the hotel room for the day on Sunday, July 17th, Zelgadis returned to the room with his friend Clayton to search for a jacket he had left behind, and then stole an envelope accidentally left on a bed (containing $6,000 in pot bonuses from Sabergaming), returning to Clayton's home to store the money there. After the rest of the crew accused Zelgadis of stealing the envelope when they found it was missing, Zelgadis reportedly lied to them, repeatedly saying that he had not stolen the envelope. After police involvement and almost four hours of investigation, Zelgadis admitted that he had stolen the envelope, and all of the cash was returned. The incident severely damaged Zelgadis's reputation, as well as the reputations of DBR and the Smash community as a whole.

   [47]Mew2King also [48]received a red card under the Unity Ruleset Infraction System at this tournament, for "[49]intentional under-performance", when he chose [50]Ganondorf in a Brawl pool match against [51]Sade.

Ruleset[[67]edit]

   GENESIS 2 featured two rulesets for Melee and Brawl. Modifications involving turning on team attack and combining percents and stocks to determine ties were made for doubles.

Melee ruleset[[68]edit]

     * 4 [69]stocks
     * 8 minute [70]time limit
     * [71]Pause is set to “Off”.
     * [72]Items are set to “Off” and “None”
     * [73]Wobbling / [74]Blizzobbling is legal.
     * Any action that can prevent the game from continuing (i.e., [75]Ice Climbers' [76]freeze glitch or [77]Mewtwo's [78]Soul Stunner., etc.) will result in a forfeit of that match for the player that initiated the action.
     * Modified [79]Dave's Stupid Rule: No player can [80]counterpick the [81]stage he or she last won on unless agreed upon by both players.
     * The act of [82]stalling is banned.
     * [83]Controller mods of any kind ([84]short hop mod, [85]L-trigger mod, etc), except for cosmetic changes (paint job, different colored plastic), are banned. Those caught with a modded controller are disqualified without a refund.
     * Turbo or programmed buttons of any kind are banned.
     * In the event of a match going to [86]Sudden Death, the winner will be determined by [87]percentage. If the above is tied, a rematch will be held.

Brawl ruleset[[88]edit]

   GENESIS 2 used the [89]Unity Ruleset for Brawl.
     * 3 Stocks
     * 8 minute time limit
     * Items are set to “Off” and “None”
     * All [90]infinite’s and [91]chain grabs are legal.
     * [92]Meta Knight's [93]Infinite Cape glitch and Extended Cape glitch are banned.
     * There is a [94]ledge grab limit of 35 for Meta Knight and 50 for all other characters. If a game goes to time and one player goes over the ledge grab limit for their character, they will automatically lose the game. If both players exceed their ledge grab limit, then this rule is ignored.
     * The act of stalling is banned.
     * If time runs out, the winner of the match will be the one called out by the game. If it goes to Sudden Death, then it is determined by percentage. If the percentage and/or both players die at the same time as a result of a [95]suicide move, a one stock 3 minute tie break match is held. Ledge grab limits are reduced to 13 for Meta Knight and 18 for all other characters.

Infraction system[[96]edit]

   GENESIS 2 followed the [97]Unity infraction system. [98]Mew2King [99]received a red card for intentional underperformance in his pools set against smasher Sade, following his yellow card for bracket manipulation at KTAR 5.

Crews[[100]edit]

   Due to the lack of crews from region specific areas, crew battles for both Melee and Brawl were extended to West Coast vs East Coast. West Coast ended up winning both crew battles, with [101]Gnes KO'ing [102]Atomsk in the [103]Brawl match and [104]Mango taking out [105]Dr. PeePee in the [106]Melee match.
