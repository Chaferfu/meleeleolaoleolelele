   Info
   Dates July 24th-25th, 2010
   Venue Fuddruckers
   Address/City 975 Diamond Blvd # E260
   Concord, California
   Attendance 84
   Entry fees Venue: $10

   [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee winners [10]USA  [11]Mew2King 1v1
   [12]USA  [13]Mew2King & [14]USA  [15]Zhu 2v2

   Good Shit German was a [16]Melee tournament held in Concord, California. It had an attendance of 84 participants and was held on July 24th-25th, 2010. It included some of the top American Melee players at the time such as [17]Mew2King, [18]Mango (Scorpion Master), [19]Shroomed, [20]SilentSpectre and [21]Lucky.
