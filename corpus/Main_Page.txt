   [12]Super Smash Bros Ultimate Box Art.png

   Super Smash Bros. Ultimate (大乱闘スマッシュブラザーズ SPECIAL, Great Fray Smash Brothers Special) is a fighting game for the [13]Nintendo Switch. It was first announced on March 8th, 2018 at the end of the [14]Nintendo Direct released the same day. It is the fifth installment in the [15]Super Smash Bros. series (sixth if both versions of [16]Super Smash Bros. 4 are counted as two games). The game was released on December 7, 2018. ([17]more...)

   29 January 2019 [72]Super Smash Bros. Ultimate
          [73]Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is updated to version [74]2.0.0, including the release of [75]Piranha Plant, the addition of multiplayer on the [76]Spirit Board, and [77]balance adjustments.

   20 January 2019 [78]Super Smash Bros. Ultimate
          At [79]Glitch 6, [80]Tweek, [81]Nairo, and [82]Light take 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place respectively in [83]Ultimate singles. [84]MkLeo & [85]Ally take [86]Ultimate doubles.

   12 January 2019 [87]Super Smash Bros. Ultimate
          At [88]Umebura SP 2, [89]Zackray, [90]Shuton, and [91]HIKARU take 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place respectively in [92]Ultimate singles.

   6 January 2019 [93]Super Smash Bros. Ultimate [94]Super Smash Bros. Melee [95]Super Smash Bros.
          At [96]Smash Conference United, [97]MkLeo, [98]Samsora, and [99]Salem take 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place respectively in [100]Ultimate singles; [101]Hungrybox takes 1st place in [102]Melee singles; [103]Loto takes 1st place in [104]Smash 64 singles. [105]Dark Wizzy & [106]Salem take [107]Ultimate doubles; [108]DaShizWiz & [109]Blea Gelo take [110]Melee doubles.
