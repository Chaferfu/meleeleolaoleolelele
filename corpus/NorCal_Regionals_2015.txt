   NorCal Regionals 2015, or simply NCR 2015, was the thirteenth edition of the [30]NorCal Regionals tournament series, a fighting game tournament held annually in northern California, organized by Terry "Kineda" Ng and famous [31]Street Fighter player John Choi. The tournament took place on April 3rd-5th, 2015, at the Holiday Inn Sacramento Capitol Plaza.^[32][1]^[33][2] The series is known to be focused on Capcom games, but a variety of fighting games were featured on its official lineup, including both [34]Melee and [35]Smash for Wii U which were listed as main events, and [36]Project M, picked throught the Community Choice Games voting.
