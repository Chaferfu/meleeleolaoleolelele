   Northwest Majors VII
   Info
   Dates April 24th-26th, 2015
   Venue Highline Community College
   Address/City 2400 S 240th St
   Des Moines, Washington [12]USA
   Entry fees Melee singles: $15
   Melee doubes: $10 per person
   Project M singles: $10
   Project M doubles: $10 per person
   Smash Wii U singles: $10
   SSB64 singles: $10
   Results
   [13]Super Smash Bros. winners [14]USA  [15]Kefit
   [16]Super Smash Bros. Melee winners [17]USA  [18]Silent Wolf 1v1
   [19]USA  [20]SFAT & [21]USA  [22]PewPewU 2v2
   [23]PM Icon.png winners [24]USA  [25]Chillin 1v1
   [26]USA  [27]SFAT & [28]USA  [29]PewPewU 2v2
   [30]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U winners [31]USA  [32]Cacogen

   Northwest Majors VII was a tournament held in Washington. It featured many top players from the West Coast and Canada.
