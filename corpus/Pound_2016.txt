   Pound 2016, also known as Pound VI, was the sixth main event in the [38]Pound tournament series created by [39]Plank, and the first major Pound tournament since [40]Pound V in 2011. The event took place in McLean, Virginia from April 2-3, 2016. Tournaments were hosted by [41]Plank in conjunction with [42]Nintendude and [43]TheTantalus, from the [44]VGBootCamp team.^[45][1]

Summary[[60]edit]

   Plank, Pound 2016's tournament organizer, was the only tournament organizer featured on the Tournament Organizer Blacklist due to his inability to pay out players at Pound V in 2011.^[61][2]^[62][3] Plank has since been removed from the blacklist, after several players reported receiving payment from Pound V.^[63][4]^[64][5]^[65][6] Registration was officially opened on December 4th, 2015.

   Pound 2016 currently stands as [66]one of the largest Smash Bros tournaments to date, with 933 entrants for [67]Melee and 514 entrants (the maximum number) for [68]Smash 4.

   With [69]ZeRo's absence from the Smash 4 event due to medical issues, [70]Abadango won his first major American tournament using [71]Mewtwo, a character generally considered to be mid [72]tier at the time. [73]Dabuz failed to make top eight while [74]Nairo was eliminated early on at a disappointing 49th place, his worst placing at a major so far. [75]Marss and [76]Pink Fresh also notably placed 3rd and 7th, respectively. In the Melee event, [77]Hungrybox took his third major in a row without dropping a set, losing only a single game to [78]Mango in grand finals; Mango additionally fell to [79]Hax in winners' semis, though he later ousted him in losers' finals. [80]Swedish Delight and [81]Laudandus both made unexpected top eight finishes while [82]InfiniteNumbers placed 9th at his first-ever major, beating [83]DJ Nintendo, [84]Alex19, [85]MacD, and [86]Mike Haze; [87]Axe and [88]Westballz also suffered early losses, placing 25th and 33rd respectively.

Trivia[[926]edit]

     * The 512 player cap for Smash 4 was exceeded by 1 due to a player from the rewards shop being added after the cap had been hit.
     * Pound 2016 stands as [927]Nairo's worst placing in Smash 4, at 49th place.
     * This was the first Smash 4 American major won by a Japanese player, Abadango.
          + This also means it was the first Smash 4 American major not won by [928]ZeRo or Nairo.
          + This was also the first Smash 4 national tournament to be won with [929]Mewtwo in grand finals.
