   R3: Revival, Redemption, Rape (or just R³) was a regional Melee, 64, and Brawl tournament in San Francisco, California on December 19th–20th, 2009. R³ was the second and final regional of the CGC @ SFSU period. Hosted by [24]Onski it was the first tournament to break 200 entrants and feature all three Smash Bros. titles.
