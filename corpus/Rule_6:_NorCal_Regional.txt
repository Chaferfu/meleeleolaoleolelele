   Rule 6: NorCal Regional
   Info
   Dates July 29th, 2012
   Venue Fuddruckers
   Address/City 1975 Diamond Blvd, E-260
   Concord, California
   Attendance 92
   Entry fees $5 venue $10 singles $20 per team
   Pot size(s) $920 singles $540 teams
   Results
   [9]Super Smash Bros. Melee winners [10]USA  [11]Mango <1v1>
   [12]USA  [13]SFAT and [14]USA  [15]Shroomed <2v2>

   Rule 6 is a NorCal regional [16]Super Smash Bros. Melee tournament. Many OOS talent appeared at the tournament like [17]Silent Wolf, [18]Bladewise, [19]Kels, [20]Tai, and [21]KirbyKaze. Mango had a notable losers run. After losing winners round 1 to Bladewise he defeated Kels, [22]Zhu, [23]Lucky, [24]S2J, [25]PewPewU, [26]Shroomed, and KirbyKaze in 2 sets of grand finals to take the tournament.
