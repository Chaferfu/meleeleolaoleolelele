   SSS: Blood For Blood was a [22]Super Smash Bros. Melee regional tournament that was held in San Francisco, California on October 15th, 2016. It was part of the Southern California tournament series [23]Super Smash Sundays. The name is a reference to the SoCal-NorCal rivalry, and how SoCal seeks 'revenge' after multiple losses against NorCal top players such as [24]SFAT and [25]PewPewU.
