   Smash 'N' Splash 3 was a [47]national tournament in Lake Delton, Wisconsin that took place on June 2nd-4th, 2017. It was the third event in the 2017 [48]Project M Championship Circuit. It was also the fifth largest [49]Project M tournament and twelfth largest [50]Melee tournament of [51]all time.

Trivia[[973]edit]

     * This tournament marks the third time in a row [974]Ally has won Smash 'N' Splash [975]Smash 4 doubles.
     * This tournament is the first tournament since [976]Get On My Level 2016 where [977]Armada was in attendance and did not make it to grand finals.
     * This was the first time in the history of [978]Project M that two [979]Ice Climbers players placed in top 8 at a national.
