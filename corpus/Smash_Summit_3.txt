   Smash Summit 3 was the third edition of the Smash Summit tournament series. It was a [19]national [20]Super Smash Bros. Melee invitational [21]tournament held in Los Angeles, California from November 3rd to 6th, 2016 by Beyond the Summit. Singles, doubles and side events were hosted. The tournament was held at the Summit gaming house, a private residence, and was not open to the general public.

Players voted in[[59]edit]

   6 more players were voted in by the community. They had to be Top 50 in the [60]2015 SSBMRank, or have placed top 16 at [61]The Big House 6, [62]Get On My Level 2016, [63]CEO 2016, [64]Super Smash Con 2016 or [65]Heir 3. The players voted in were, in that order:
     * [66]USA  [67]Mafia & [68]USA  [69]S2J (October 19th)
     * [70]USA  [71]Duck & [72]USA  [73]PewPewU (October 20th)
     * [74]Canada  [75]n0ne & [76]USA  [77]The Moon (October 21st)

Around the World[[435]edit]

   Around the World is a 4-player game where each player starts with a [436]character from a corner of the select screen and advances characters by winning a round. Each round consists of a 2 stock, 2 minute random stage free-for-all with items on--sudden deaths are played out. Player who advances the most characters in a set time or travels around the world wins.

Trivia[[493]edit]

     * This is the only major invitational tournament in 2016 to not have an Ice Climbers main in it.
     * This is the first tournament in the Smash Summit tournament series where a participating member of the Top Six did not make top eight, with Leffen's placing at 9th.
