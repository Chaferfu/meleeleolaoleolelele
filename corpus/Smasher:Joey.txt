   Joey is a [19]Smash 4 player from Louisville who mains [20]Rosalina & Luma, but plays a lot of characters at a tournament level. He has win(s) over [21]Wizzrobe, [22]Katakiri, [23]Darkshad, and [24]Absol. He is [25]JDB's older brother. He was formerly ranked 1st on the Smash 4 [26]Kentucky Power Rankings.
