                          Additional info
      Real name     Nicholas M. Whittier
       Location     Northern California [13] United States

   NMW is a [14]Melee [15]Captain Falcon player from Northern California. He is currently ranked 7th on the [16]NorCal Melee Power Rankings and 83rd on the [17]2018 MPGR. NMW is well known for his doubles gameplay as well as creating a beginners guide for doubles. He is a former WarCraft III professional and went by the screen name "Shriek".
