   Super Smash Con 2016 was the 2nd edition of the "Super Smash Con", a video game convention and national tournament focused on the [46]Super Smash Bros. series, held in Chantilly, Virginia, from August 11th-14th, 2016. The event hosted singles and doubles tournaments for all four Smash games, and is currently [47]the largest Smash 64 tournament to date and the fifth largest Smash Wii U tournament of all time. Because of this, it is the biggest Smash For Wii U tournament that only had Smash games and/or was not part of the [48]EVO Series.^[49][1]

   Rules: All 4 Smash games are played in a best-of-three format (5 stocks for Smash 64, 4 for Melee, 3 for Brawl, and 2 for Smash 4). A big victory is earned by winning a game with a high enough amount of stocks left (3 for Smash 64/Melee and 2 for Brawl/Smash 4).

   The winner is determined
     * first by sets won
     * second by games won
     * third by "big victories".

Trivia[[1303]edit]

     * [1304]TSM-sponsored smashers [1305]Leffen and [1306]ZeRo, both of whom had respectively won [1307]Super Smash Con 2015 in Melee and Smash 4, notably failed to defend their titles.
          + [1308]Leffen could not defend his Melee title in any capacity, due to his ongoing visa issues surrounding his sponsor.
          + [1309]ZeRo finished in 13th after losing to [1310]Day in the winner's bracket and [1311]Ally in the loser's bracket. This finish stood as his worst placing in a Smash 4 tournament until his 49th place finish at [1312]2GGC: Civil War.
     * [1313]Mew2King also failed to defend his title in Brawl singles, losing to [1314]V115 in winner's bracket and [1315]Ally in loser's bracket, finishing at 9th place.
