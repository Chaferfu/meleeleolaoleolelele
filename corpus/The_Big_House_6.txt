   The Big House 6 was a national [28]Super Smash Bros. Melee and [29]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U tournament held in Dearborn, Michigan from October 7th-9th, 2016. It was the sixth edition of [30]Juggleguy's prestigious Midwest [31]tournament series.

   The competition was fierce for both games. In Melee, the four active [32]Gods [33]Armada, [34]Hungrybox, [35]Mango, and [36]Mew2King were present, as well as all the other American top players such as [37]Plup, [38]Axe, [39]SFAT, [40]Wizzrobe, and [41]PewPewU. Europe was also well represented with [42]Ice, [43]Professor Pro, and [44]Android participating. The Big House 6 was notable for having high-level international attendees such as [45]Rudolph from Japan and [46]Spud from New Zealand. It was also [47]Leffen's first tournament in the United States in over a year, although he ended up [48]sandbagging.

   In [49]Smash 4, 27 of the 50 [50]Panda Global Rankings v1 ranked players were in attendance, including the defending champion [51]ZeRo, the [52]EVO 2016 champion [53]Ally, [54]Nairo, [55]Dabuz, [56]ANTi, [57]Mr.R, [58]VoiD, [59]Abadango, [60]komorikiri, [61]Larry Lurr and many others. Unranked threats such as [62]Kameme (who had recently placed 2nd at EVO) and [63]Salem (who had made his breakthrough in the top echelon of players at [64]Collision XIV) were additional contenders for 1st place.

   The Big House 6 is the [65]seventh largest Smash tournament of all time. It currently stands as the [66]fifth largest Melee tournament of all time as well as the [67]eleventh largest Smash 4 tournament.

Trivia[[1020]edit]

     * The Big House 6 is the first US tournament [1021]Leffen was able to enter since [1022]HTC Throwdown one year before due to issues receiving a worker's visa. As a result, he decided [1023]not to take singles seriously and opted to play Marth and Falco, getting 17th place.
     * This tournament is notable for the many upsets that occurred during Super Smash Bros. Melee's bracket, including [1024]Silent Wolf's victory over [1025]Mew2King, [1026]Zain's victory over [1027]Plup, and [1028]R2DLiu's victory over [1029]Axe.
