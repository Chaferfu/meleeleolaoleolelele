   The Holiday Bash Smash Invitational was an invitational [20]Super Smash Bros. Melee tournament held on December 15th-16th, 2017 and featured a large prize pot provided by Twitch.tv. The event, specifically the [21]crew battles, was inspired by the East Coast/West Coast rivalries. The singles event featured 2 groups followed by a double elimination format.
