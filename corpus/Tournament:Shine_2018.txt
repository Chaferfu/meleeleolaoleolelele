Trivia[[1120]edit]

     * This tournament stands as [1121]CaptainZack's worst placing since his 97th place finish at [1122]CEO 2016, placing 49th losing to [1123]Arhungry and [1124]Kuma.
          + CaptainZack announced his retirement from [1125]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U singles after his elimination from the tournament, but clarified that he would continue to participate in doubles events. He later announced that he would continue playing Smash Wii U singles as a Peach main however.
     * This is [1126]Zain's first win at a major as well as the first major win in Melee for someone in the [1127]MD/VA area since [1128]Azen at [1129]Viva La Smashtaclysm in 2007. In addition, Zain became the first [1130]"documentary kid" to win a major.
     * This is the first time that [1131]Hungrybox has lost to a [1132]Marth player since [1133]PewPewU at [1134]Apex 2015.
     * In response to the Jacksonville shooting earlier that day, a moment of silence was held before Super Smash Bros. Melee grand finals.
