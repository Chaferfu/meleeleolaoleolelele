   The Big House 8 was a national [36]Super Smash Bros., [37]Super Smash Bros Melee, and [38]Super Smash Bros. for Wii U tournament that took place on October 5th-7th, 2018 in Detroit, [39]Michigan. The event was hosted by [40]Juggleguy and was held inside Detroit's Cobo Center.

Trivia[[768]edit]

     * This tournament marks the first ever win of a [769]Corrin main at a national tournament, with [770]Cosmos taking the Wii U singles event.
          + This tournament also marks the highest ever placing of a [771]Toon Link main at a national with [772]yeti placing 4th.
     * This tournament marks [773]Hungrybox's first supermajor win in 2018 despite being considered the best player during most of the year taking the Melee singles event.
     * [774]Plup made an impressive run through losers bracket after losing to [775]Bananas 0-3 in the start of Top 64, defeating [776]moky, [777]n0ne, [778]Lucky, [779]La Luna, [780]Swedish Delight, [781]Axe, [782]Leffen, [783]Zain, and [784]Mango before losing to [785]Hungrybox in Grand Finals, placing 2nd at the tournament.
     * This tournament stands as [786]PewPewU's worst performance in a [787]Melee tournament since [788]GENESIS, placing 33rd.
     * This is the first time [789]Mango was unable to win an even-numbered Big House tournament, as he won [790]The Big House 2, [791]The Big House 4 and [792]The Big House 6.
