   The Roast of Hugo Gonzalez was a super regional [20]Super Smash Bros. Melee tournament held in Long Beach, California, on September 22nd, 2018. It was the first open, non-invitational tournament organized by the Beyond the Summit crew. The event was dedicated towards [21]HugS, and featured a special live roast event before the top 8.
